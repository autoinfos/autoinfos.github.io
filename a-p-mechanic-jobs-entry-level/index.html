What is A&P Certification?

A&P certification is a designation given to individuals who have completed a Federal Aviation Administration (FAA)-approved training program and passed both written and practical exams. This certification is required for anyone who wants to work as an A&P mechanic.

How Long Does it Take to Obtain A&P Certification?

The length of time it takes to obtain A&P certification can vary depending on the program you choose. Most programs take between 18 months and two years to complete. However, some accelerated programs may take as little as 12 months.
