What to Expect in AC Technician Jobs in Dubai Airport

As an AC technician in Dubai airport, you'll be responsible for maintaining and repairing the airport's HVAC systems. This includes inspecting and testing systems to identify potential problems, troubleshooting issues, and repairing or replacing faulty components.

I had to work quickly and efficiently to identify the problem and replace the faulty component to ensure that the tower's air conditioning system was working properly.

Other responsibilities of AC technicians in Dubai airport may include installing new HVAC systems, performing routine maintenance, and keeping accurate records of all work performed.

Qualifications Needed for AC Technician Jobs in Dubai Airport

To work as an AC technician in Dubai airport, you'll need to have a high school diploma or equivalent and have completed a technical training program in HVAC systems. Additionally, you'll need to have several years of experience in the field and possess a valid UAE driver's license.
