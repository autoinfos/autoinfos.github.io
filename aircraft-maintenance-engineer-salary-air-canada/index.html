Aircraft Maintenance Engineer Salary Air Canada: Understanding the Target

As mentioned earlier, the average salary for an aircraft maintenance engineer with Air Canada is around $70,000 to $100,000 per year. However, this can vary based on several factors, including:

Firstly, geographical location can impact the salary of an aircraft maintenance engineer. For instance, those working in high-cost cities like Toronto or Vancouver may earn more than those in smaller cities.

Secondly, the type of aircraft and level of experience can also affect the salary. Those who work with larger and more complex aircraft may earn more than those who work with smaller aircraft.

Lastly, the level of seniority can also play a role in the salary. Those who have been with the company for a longer time and have more experience may earn a higher salary compared to those who are new to the industry.
