So, what exactly is an anesthesia technician? An anesthesia technician is a healthcare professional who assists anesthesiologists and nurse anesthetists in the administration of anesthesia during medical procedures. Their responsibilities include preparing and maintaining anesthesia equipment, monitoring patients during anesthesia, and providing support to the anesthesia team.
