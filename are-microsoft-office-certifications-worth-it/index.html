Are Microsoft Office Certifications Worth It

Written by Daniel Aug 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you been considering getting a Microsoft Office certification? Are you wondering if it's worth the time and money? In this blog post, we'll explore the benefits of Microsoft Office certifications and help you decide if they are worth pursuing.

Many professionals use Microsoft Office on a daily basis, but not all of them are proficient in all of its features. This can lead to frustration and inefficiency in the workplace. A Microsoft Office certification can help you stand out from your peers and demonstrate your expertise in using the software.

So, are Microsoft Office certifications worth it? The short answer is yes. A Microsoft Office certification can boost your resume, increase your earning potential, and improve your job prospects. Let's dive deeper into the benefits of Microsoft Office certifications.

Firstly, a Microsoft Office certification shows that you have mastered a specific set of skills. This can be very attractive to potential employers and can help you stand out in a crowded job market. Additionally, many companies require or prefer candidates with Microsoft Office certifications for certain positions.

Another benefit of Microsoft Office certifications is the potential for increased earning potential. According to a survey by Global Knowledge, professionals with Microsoft Office certifications earn up to 16% more than their non-certified peers. This can add up to a significant increase in salary over time.

Personal Experience: How Microsoft Office Certifications Helped Me

When I first started in my current job, I was proficient in Microsoft Word and Excel, but I hadn't really explored some of the other programs in the Microsoft Office suite. However, when I saw that my company was offering a training program that would lead to a certification, I decided to enroll.

The training was challenging, but I learned so much about the other Microsoft Office programs like PowerPoint and Access. After I received my certification, I felt more confident in my abilities and was able to take on more complex projects at work. I also received a small raise as a result of my certification.

How to Get a Microsoft Office Certification

Getting a Microsoft Office certification is relatively easy and affordable. There are many online courses and training programs that can help you prepare for the certification exam. Microsoft also offers official certification exams that you can take at a testing center.

Before you pursue a certification, it's important to determine which program(s) you want to become certified in. There are individual certifications for each program, as well as more advanced certifications that cover multiple programs.

Types of Microsoft Office Certifications

There are several types of Microsoft Office certifications, including:

  • MOS: Microsoft Office Specialist
  • MOS Expert
  • MOS Master
  • Microsoft Certified: Microsoft Office Specialist

How to Prepare for a Microsoft Office Certification Exam

There are many resources available to help you prepare for a Microsoft Office certification exam. Microsoft offers official study guides and practice exams on their website. Additionally, there are many online courses and training programs that can help you master the skills needed to pass the exam.

Is a Microsoft Office Certification Right for You?

If you use Microsoft Office on a regular basis and want to demonstrate your expertise, a Microsoft Office certification is definitely worth considering. It can help you stand out in a crowded job market and increase your earning potential. Additionally, the skills you learn while preparing for the certification can help you become more efficient and effective in your job.

Question and Answer

Q: Do I need to have a certain level of proficiency in Microsoft Office before pursuing a certification?

A: It's recommended that you have a basic understanding of the program(s) you want to become certified in before pursuing a certification. However, there are many resources available to help you improve your skills and prepare for the certification exam.

Q: How much does a Microsoft Office certification cost?

A: The cost of a certification exam varies depending on the program and your location. However, most exams cost between $100 and $150.

Q: How long does it take to prepare for a Microsoft Office certification exam?

A: The amount of time it takes to prepare for a certification exam varies depending on your current level of proficiency and how much time you can dedicate to studying. However, most people can prepare for the exam in a few weeks to a few months.

Q: How long is a Microsoft Office certification valid?

A: Microsoft Office certifications are valid for three years. After three years, you will need to recertify to maintain your certification.

Conclusion of Are Microsoft Office Certifications Worth It?

In conclusion, Microsoft Office certifications are definitely worth considering if you use the software on a regular basis. They can help you stand out in a competitive job market, increase your earning potential, and improve your job prospects. With many affordable online courses and training programs available, there's no reason not to pursue a certification.
