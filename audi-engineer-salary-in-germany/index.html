Additionally, some engineers report feeling undervalued or underpaid for their work, which can lead to job dissatisfaction.

So, how much can you expect to make as an Audi engineer in Germany? The target salary for an entry-level engineer is around €48,000 per year. However, it's essential to note that salaries can vary widely depending on factors such as location, company size, and industry.
