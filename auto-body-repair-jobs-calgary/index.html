Types of Auto Body Repair Jobs in Calgary

There are several types of auto body repair jobs in Calgary. These include:

  • Dent removal
  • Body repair
  • Paint matching
  • Frame straightening
  • Windshield repair or replacement

Each of these jobs requires a different set of skills and techniques. For example, dent removal involves using specialized tools to remove dents from the body of the car. Body repair involves repairing any damage to the body of the car, such as scratches or dents. Paint matching involves matching the color of the new paint to the existing paint on the car. Frame straightening involves using hydraulic tools to straighten any damage to the frame of the car.
