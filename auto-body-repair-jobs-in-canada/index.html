Auto Body Repair Jobs in Canada: The Process

Auto body repair jobs in Canada involve several steps to ensure that the vehicle is repaired correctly and efficiently. The following are the typical steps involved in the auto body repair process:

Step 1: Assessment

The technician assesses the damage to the vehicle and creates an estimate for the repairs. The estimate includes the cost of parts and labor required to repair the damage.

Step 2: Disassembly

The technician disassembles the damaged parts to assess the extent of the damage. They also inspect the vehicle's frame to ensure that it is not bent or damaged.

Step 3: Repair

The technician repairs the damaged parts using various tools and techniques, such as welding, painting, and body filler. They also replace any damaged parts with new ones.

Step 4: Reassembly

The technician reassembles the repaired parts and ensures that the vehicle looks and functions as good as new.
