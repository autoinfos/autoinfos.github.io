FAQs About Auto Body Repair Near Me Now

Q: How long does it take to repair a car after an accident?

A: The length of time it takes to repair a car after an accident depends on the extent of the damage. Minor repairs can take a few days, while more extensive damage can take several weeks.

Q: Will my insurance cover the cost of auto body repairs?

A: It depends on your insurance policy and the circumstances of the accident. Most insurance policies will cover the cost of auto body repairs, but you may need to pay a deductible.

Q: Can I choose which auto body repair service to use?

A: Yes, you have the right to choose which auto body repair service you use. However, your insurance company may have a list of preferred providers or may require you to use a certain service.

Q: Can I drive my car after an accident?
