What to Look for in an Auto Body Shop in Canada

Question and Answer Section

Q: How long does it take to repair a car at an auto body shop in Canada?

A: The time it takes to repair a car at an auto body shop in Canada depends on the extent of the damage. Minor repairs can take a few days, while major repairs can take several weeks.

Q: Will my insurance cover the cost of repairs at an auto body shop in Canada?

A: If you have collision coverage, your insurance should cover the cost of repairs at an auto body shop in Canada. However, you may have to pay a deductible.

Q: Can I choose which auto body shop to take my car to?

A: Yes, you have the right to choose which auto body shop to take your car to. Your insurance company may recommend a shop, but you are not required to use their recommendation.
