Understanding Auto Body Technician Salary per Hour

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median hourly wage for auto body technicians was $22.76 as of May 2020. However, wages can range from $12.10 to $40.83 per hour depending on factors such as location, experience, and type of employer. Auto body technicians who work in metropolitan areas tend to earn higher wages than those in rural areas.

The Factors That Impact Auto Body Technician Salary per Hour

As mentioned, several factors can impact your earnings as an auto body technician. Experience is one of the most significant factors, with those who have more experience typically earning higher wages. Additionally, those who work for larger companies or dealerships tend to earn more than those who work for smaller independent shops.

How to Increase Your Earnings as an Auto Body Technician

If you're looking to increase your earnings as an auto body technician, there are several things you can do.
