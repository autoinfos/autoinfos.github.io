What Does the Job Entail?

As an auto mechanic, you will be responsible for diagnosing and repairing various automotive issues. This could range from simple maintenance tasks like changing oil and replacing brakes to more complex repairs like engine rebuilds and transmission replacements.

You will also be responsible for communicating with customers and explaining the repairs needed and their associated costs. You will work with a team of experienced mechanics and will have access to the latest tools and technology to help you perform your job efficiently.

Skills and Qualifications Required

To be considered for our auto mechanic job vacancy, you must have a high school diploma or equivalent qualification. You must also have post-secondary training in automotive technology and have at least two years of experience working in a garage or dealership.

You must have a valid driver's license and have the ability to work in a fast-paced environment. You must also be a team player and have excellent communication skills.
