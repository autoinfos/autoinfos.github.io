Let's face it - auto repair shops are not the most pleasant places to be. They are often loud, dirty, and filled with the smell of oil and gasoline. But for many people, taking their car to the shop is a necessary evil. That's why we wanted to create a space that would make customers feel comfortable and at ease.

Our goal was to create an environment that would make customers forget that they were in an auto repair shop. We wanted to create a space that felt more like a living room than a garage. To achieve this, we focused on a few key elements.

First, we added comfortable seating areas where customers could relax while they waited for their car to be serviced. We used warm colors and soft fabrics to create a cozy atmosphere. We also added plants and artwork to make the space feel more homey.

We also had to consider the practical needs of an auto repair shop. We needed to make sure that the space was easy to clean and maintain, and that it was durable enough to withstand the wear and tear of daily use.
