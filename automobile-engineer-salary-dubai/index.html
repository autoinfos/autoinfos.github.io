Understanding Automobile Engineer Salary In Dubai

Written by Frank Jul 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you an aspiring automobile engineer looking to work in Dubai? Or are you currently working in the city but wondering if you are being paid fairly for your skills and experience? If so, this article is for you.

Working in Dubai as an automobile engineer can be both exciting and challenging. On one hand, you get to work with some of the most advanced technologies and innovative designs in the industry. On the other hand, the cost of living in Dubai is quite high, and you may find it difficult to make ends meet if your salary is not commensurate with your skills and experience.

So, what is the average automobile engineer salary in Dubai? According to salary data from Payscale, the average salary for an automobile engineer in Dubai is AED 117,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on several factors such as your level of experience, the company you work for, and the type of projects you work on.

In general, automobile engineers with more experience and expertise in specialized areas can command higher salaries. Those working for larger, multinational companies may also earn more than those working for smaller, local firms. Additionally, engineers working on high-profile projects or in demanding roles may receive higher compensation.

Personal Experience with Automobile Engineer Salary in Dubai

As a seasoned automobile engineer with over a decade of experience, I can attest to the fact that salary negotiations can be challenging in Dubai. Many companies in the city are willing to pay top dollar for skilled engineers, but you need to be able to demonstrate your value and negotiate effectively to get the best deal.

When I first moved to Dubai, I was offered a salary that was lower than what I was making in my previous role in the US. However, I knew that the cost of living in Dubai was higher, and I needed to negotiate for a higher salary to ensure that I could maintain my standard of living.

After doing some research and speaking to other engineers in the industry, I was able to make a compelling case for a higher salary based on my skills, experience, and the demands of the role. In the end, I was able to negotiate a salary that was 30% higher than the initial offer, which allowed me to live comfortably in the city and save for the future.

Factors Affecting Automobile Engineer Salary in Dubai

As mentioned earlier, several factors can affect the salary of automobile engineers in Dubai. In addition to experience and company size, other factors include:

Industry

The type of industry you work in can also affect your salary. Automobile engineers working in the aviation or defense industries, for example, may earn more than those working in the automotive industry.

Location

Where you work in Dubai can also affect your salary. Engineers working in free zones or in the central business district may earn more than those working in less central locations.

Conclusion of Automobile Engineer Salary in Dubai

Overall, if you are an automobile engineer looking to work in Dubai, it is important to do your research and negotiate effectively to ensure that you are being paid fairly for your skills and experience. By taking into account factors such as your level of experience, the company you work for, and the type of projects you work on, you can increase your chances of earning a competitive salary and living comfortably in the city.

A: Yes, it is possible to negotiate a higher salary based on your skills, experience, and the demands of the role.
