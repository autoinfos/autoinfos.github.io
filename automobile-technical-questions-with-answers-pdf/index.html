Question and Answer Section

Q: How often should I rotate my tires?

A: You should rotate your tires every 5,000 to 7,500 miles. This will help ensure that your tires wear evenly and last longer.

Q: What is the difference between synthetic and conventional oil?

A: Synthetic oil is designed to last longer and perform better in extreme temperatures. Conventional oil is typically less expensive but needs to be changed more frequently.

Q: Can I use regular gasoline in a car that requires premium?

A: It is not recommended to use regular gasoline in a car that requires premium. This can cause engine damage and decrease performance.

Q: How often should I replace my car's battery?

A: Car batteries typically last 3-5 years. It is important to have your battery checked by a mechanic regularly and to replace it when necessary.
