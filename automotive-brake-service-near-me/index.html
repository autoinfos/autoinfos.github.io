How to Maintain Your Brake System

Here are some tips for maintaining your brake system:

  • Check your brake pads and rotors for wear regularly.
  • Replace brake pads and rotors as soon as they show signs of wear.
  • Keep your brake system clean and free of debris.
  • Use high-quality brake fluid and replace it every two years.

FAQs About Automotive Brake Service Near Me

Q: How often should I get my brakes checked?

A: It's recommended to have your brakes checked at least once a year or every 12,000 miles.

Q: How do I know if my brakes need to be serviced?

A: Look out for signs such as squeaky brakes, longer stopping distances, or a grinding noise when applying the brakes. These are all indicators that it's time for a brake service.

Q: How long does a brake service take?

A: A brake service can take anywhere from 1-3 hours, depending on the severity of the issue.

Q: Can I drive my car if my brakes are grinding?

A: It's not recommended to drive your car if your brakes are grinding. This could be a sign of worn brake pads or rotors, which can cause further damage to your brake system if left unchecked.
