What are the Qualifications Needed for Automotive Engineering Jobs in Nairobi?

To pursue a career in automotive engineering in Nairobi, you will typically need a bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering or a related field. Some employers may also require a master's degree or additional certifications. It's important to gain relevant experience through internships or entry-level positions, as well as to have a strong understanding of engineering principles and computer-aided design (CAD) software.
