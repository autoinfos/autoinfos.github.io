Types of Automotive Jobs in Jacksonville FL

There are many different types of automotive jobs available in Jacksonville FL. Here are a few examples:

1. Automotive Technician:

As an automotive technician, you will be responsible for repairing and maintaining vehicles. This job requires a strong knowledge of automotive systems and the ability to diagnose and fix problems.

How to become an automotive technician:

To become an automotive technician, you will need to complete a training program and obtain certification. There are many automotive technician programs available in Jacksonville FL, including programs at local colleges and vocational schools.

2. Car Salesperson:

As a car salesperson, you will be responsible for selling cars to customers. This job requires excellent communication skills and knowledge of different car models and features.

How to become a car salesperson:

To become a car salesperson, you will need to have a high school diploma or equivalent. Some dealerships may require additional training or certification.
