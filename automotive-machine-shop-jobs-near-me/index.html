What Are the Different Types of Automotive Machine Shop Jobs Near Me?

There are several different types of automotive machine shop jobs, each with its own set of responsibilities and requirements. Some common positions include:

Engine Machinist

Engine machinists are responsible for disassembling, cleaning, inspecting, and rebuilding engines. They use precision machinery to bore out cylinders, resurface cylinder heads, and balance crankshafts.

Transmission Technician

Transmission technicians are responsible for repairing and rebuilding transmissions. They use precision machinery to disassemble and inspect transmission components, and then rebuild them with new parts.
