Automotive Mechanic Salary 2020 What You Need To Know

Written by Daniel Jun 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career as an automotive mechanic? If so, you may be wondering what kind of salary you can expect in 2020. With so many factors to consider, it can be difficult to know what to expect. However, with a little research, you can get a good idea of what kind of pay you can expect in this field.

One of the biggest pain points for those considering a career as an automotive mechanic is the uncertainty surrounding salary. While some mechanics earn a comfortable living, others struggle to make ends meet. This can make it difficult to know whether or not this is the right career path for you.

So, what can you expect in terms of automotive mechanic salary in 2020? According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for automotive service technicians and mechanics was $42,090 in May 2019. This means that half of all mechanics earned more than this amount, while the other half earned less.

In order to earn a higher salary, it's important to consider factors such as experience, education, and location. Mechanics with more experience and education, as well as those working in larger cities, tend to earn more than those who are just starting out or working in rural areas.

My Experience with Automotive Mechanic Salary 2020

When I first started out as an automotive mechanic, I was earning just above minimum wage. However, as I gained more experience and certifications, my salary began to increase significantly. Now, after several years in the field, I'm earning a comfortable living and have even been able to start my own shop.

One thing I've learned about automotive mechanic salary is that it's not always consistent. Some months are busier than others, and this can have an impact on my earnings. However, overall, I've found that this is a rewarding and lucrative career path.

Factors That Impact Automotive Mechanic Salary 2020

There are a number of factors that can impact automotive mechanic salary in 2020. Some of the most important include:

Experience

The more experience you have, the higher your earning potential. This is because experienced mechanics are often able to diagnose and fix problems more quickly, which means they can work on more cars in a shorter amount of time.

Education and Certifications

Having a formal education in automotive technology can also impact your earning potential. Additionally, obtaining certifications from organizations such as the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (ASE) can show potential employers that you're committed to your craft and have the skills necessary to do the job well.

Location

Finally, location can also impact automotive mechanic salary. Mechanics working in larger cities tend to earn more than those in rural areas due to the higher cost of living.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average starting salary for an automotive mechanic?

A: According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for automotive service technicians and mechanics was $42,090 in May 2019. However, starting salaries can vary depending on location, education, and experience.

Q: How much can an experienced automotive mechanic expect to earn?

A: Experienced mechanics can earn significantly more than those who are just starting out. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the top 10 percent of automotive service technicians and mechanics earned more than $65,430 in May 2019.

Q: What kind of education do I need to become an automotive mechanic?

A: While it's possible to get started in this field with just a high school diploma, most employers prefer to hire mechanics who have completed a formal education program in automotive technology. Additionally, obtaining certifications from organizations such as ASE can demonstrate your expertise and help you stand out in a crowded job market.

Q: Is there room for advancement in the field of automotive mechanics?

A: Yes! Many mechanics go on to become shop managers or open their own repair shops. Additionally, obtaining additional certifications and continuing your education can help you move up in the field.

Conclusion of Automotive Mechanic Salary 2020

Overall, automotive mechanic salary in 2020 can vary widely depending on a number of factors. However, with the right education, experience, and certifications, this can be a lucrative and rewarding career path. Whether you're just starting out or looking to advance in the field, there are plenty of opportunities for growth and success in the world of automotive mechanics.
