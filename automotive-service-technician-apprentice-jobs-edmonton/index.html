What is an Automotive Service Technician Apprentice Job?

An automotive service technician apprentice job involves working with cars and other vehicles to diagnose and repair mechanical issues. This can include everything from routine maintenance tasks such as oil changes and brake replacements, to more complex repairs such as engine overhauls and transmission replacements.

As an apprentice, you will work under the supervision of a fully qualified technician, and will be responsible for learning the ropes of the trade. This may involve shadowing your mentor, attending training courses, and completing hands-on work in a repair shop or garage. Over time, as you gain more experience and knowledge, you will begin to take on more responsibility and work independently.
