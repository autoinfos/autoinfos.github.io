What Are Some Common Courses in Automotive Technician Programs?

Automotive technician courses in Ontario cover a wide range of topics, including:

Engine Repair and Maintenance

These courses cover the basics of engine mechanics, including how to diagnose and repair common problems like oil leaks, overheating, and poor performance.

Electrical and Electronics Systems

These courses teach students how to diagnose and repair common electrical problems in vehicles, including battery issues, faulty alternators, and malfunctioning sensors.

What Are Some Career Paths for Automotive Technicians?

After completing automotive technician courses in Ontario, you'll be well-equipped to pursue a variety of career paths in the automotive industry. Some common options include working as a:

Service Technician

Service technicians are responsible for diagnosing and repairing problems with vehicles, as well as performing routine maintenance and inspections.

Diagnostic Technician

Diagnostic technicians specialize in diagnosing complex problems with vehicles, using advanced tools and equipment to identify issues that may not be immediately apparent.

Shop Manager

Shop managers oversee the day-to-day operations of a repair shop or dealership, including managing staff, scheduling appointments, and ensuring that work is completed on time and within budget.
