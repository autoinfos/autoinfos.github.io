The target of automotive technician license renewal in Ontario is to ensure that all licensed technicians meet the same standards of competency and professionalism. The process includes completing mandatory courses, submitting proof of liability insurance, and passing a written exam. The renewal period is every five years, and you must submit your renewal application at least 60 days before your license expires.

What Happens If I Don't Renew My Automotive Technician License in Ontario?

If you do not renew your automotive technician license in Ontario, you cannot legally work as a licensed technician. You may face fines or legal action if you continue to work without a valid license. It is crucial to renew your license on time to avoid any complications.

The best way to prepare for the automotive technician license renewal exam in Ontario is to study the course materials provided by the Ministry of Transportation. You can also take practice exams to familiarize yourself with the format and types of questions you may encounter.
