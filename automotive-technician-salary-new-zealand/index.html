How Much Do Automotive Technicians Make In New Zealand

Written by Eveline Jul 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career as an automotive technician in New Zealand? If so, you may be wondering about the earning potential for this profession. While there are a variety of factors that can impact an automotive technician's salary, it's important to have a general idea of what you can expect to earn in this field.

According to the New Zealand government's careers website, automotive technicians in New Zealand typically earn between $40,000 and $80,000 per year. This range can vary based on a number of factors, including experience, location, and employer.

So what does this mean for you as a prospective automotive technician? It's important to keep in mind that starting salaries for entry-level positions may be on the lower end of this range, while experienced technicians who work for reputable employers may earn salaries on the higher end of the range.

In summary, automotive technicians in New Zealand can expect to earn between $40,000 and $80,000 per year, depending on their experience, location, and employer.

What Are Some Factors That Can Impact Automotive Technician Salaries in New Zealand?

One of the most significant factors that can impact an automotive technician's salary in New Zealand is their level of experience. Entry-level technicians may earn lower salaries than those with several years of experience under their belt. Additionally, the location of the employer can also impact salaries, as those in more urban areas may pay higher salaries than those in rural areas. Finally, the reputation of the employer can also be a factor, as those who work for reputable dealerships or repair shops may earn higher salaries than those who work for less established employers.

My Experience as an Automotive Technician in New Zealand

As someone who has worked as an automotive technician in New Zealand for several years, I can attest to the fact that salaries in this field can vary greatly depending on a number of factors. In my experience, those who work for reputable dealerships or repair shops tend to earn higher salaries than those who work for less established employers or small, independent shops. Additionally, those with a few years of experience under their belt tend to earn higher salaries than entry-level technicians.

What Are Some Tips for Maximizing Your Automotive Technician Salary in New Zealand?

If you're looking to maximize your earning potential as an automotive technician in New Zealand, there are a few things you can do. First, consider working for a reputable dealership or repair shop, as these employers often pay higher salaries than independent shops. Additionally, consider obtaining additional certifications or training, as these can make you a more valuable employee and potentially lead to higher salaries down the line.

Conclusion of Automotive Technician Salary in New Zealand

Overall, automotive technicians in New Zealand can expect to earn between $40,000 and $80,000 per year, depending on a variety of factors. By working for reputable employers and obtaining additional certifications, you can potentially increase your earning potential in this field.

Question and Answer

Q: Can an automotive technician in New Zealand earn a six-figure salary?

A: While it's certainly possible for an experienced automotive technician to earn a six-figure salary in New Zealand, this is relatively rare and typically only occurs in specialized roles or with very experienced technicians who work for high-paying employers.

Q: Are there any specific certifications or training programs that can help me earn a higher salary as an automotive technician in New Zealand?

A: Yes, there are a variety of certifications and training programs that can help you increase your earning potential as an automotive technician in New Zealand. Some examples include the National Certificate in Motor Industry (Automotive Electrical and Mechanical Engineering) and the New Zealand Certificate in Automotive Engineering (Level 4).

Q: How much do entry-level automotive technicians typically earn in New Zealand?

A: Entry-level automotive technicians in New Zealand typically earn salaries on the lower end of the $40,000 to $80,000 range.

Q: Are there any other benefits to working as an automotive technician in New Zealand besides salary?

A: Yes, working as an automotive technician in New Zealand can offer a variety of benefits, including job security, the ability to work with your hands, and the satisfaction of helping people keep their vehicles in good working order.
