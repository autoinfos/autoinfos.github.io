2. Program Offerings: Look for a school that offers a program that aligns with your career goals. For example, if you want to become an automotive technician, look for a school that offers a program in automotive technology.

4. Reputation: Check the school's reputation in the industry. Look for reviews and ratings from former students, as well as information about job placement rates and employer partnerships.
