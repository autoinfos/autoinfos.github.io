Factors That Can Affect Your Salary

As mentioned earlier, several factors can affect your salary as an engineer in New Zealand. These include:

Experience

Another factor that can affect your salary is your field of specialization. Some fields, such as software engineering, tend to pay higher salaries than others due to the high demand for these skills.
