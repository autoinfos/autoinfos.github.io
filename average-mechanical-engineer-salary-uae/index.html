Average Mechanical Engineer Salary In Uae

Written by Frank Sep 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a mechanical engineer in the UAE? Are you curious about how much you should be earning? You’re not alone. Knowing the average mechanical engineer salary in the UAE is important for anyone in the field. In this post, we’ll explore the topic in depth and provide you with all the information you need.

It’s no secret that the cost of living in the UAE is high. That being said, mechanical engineers are in high demand and can expect to earn a decent salary. However, it’s important to note that salaries can vary depending on a number of factors, such as experience, location, and industry.

So, what can you expect to earn as a mechanical engineer in the UAE? According to recent data, the average salary for a mechanical engineer in the UAE is around AED 96,000 per year. However, this number can range anywhere from AED 60,000 to AED 180,000 depending on the factors mentioned previously.

To summarize, the average mechanical engineer salary in the UAE is around AED 96,000 per year. However, this number can vary depending on experience, location, and industry.

Factors That Affect Mechanical Engineer Salaries in the UAE

As previously mentioned, there are a number of factors that can affect mechanical engineer salaries in the UAE. Let’s take a closer look at each one:

Experience

Experience is one of the biggest factors that affects mechanical engineer salaries in the UAE. Those with more experience can expect to earn more than those who are just starting out in their careers.

Location

Location is another factor that can affect mechanical engineer salaries in the UAE. Those working in cities like Dubai and Abu Dhabi can expect to earn more than those working in smaller cities or towns.

Industry

The industry you work in can also affect your salary as a mechanical engineer in the UAE. For example, those working in the oil and gas industry can expect to earn more than those working in other industries.
