One of the biggest pain points for medical lab technologists in Canada is the perception that their salaries are lower than those of other healthcare professionals. Answering the Target of Average Medical Lab Technologist Salary in Canada

For example, medical lab technologists who work in hospitals or research facilities may earn more than those who work in private clinics or laboratories.

What Affects the Average Medical Lab Technologist Salary in Canada?
