Best Audible Tech Books To Listen To In 2021

Written by Christine Sep 13, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you interested in technology but find yourself too busy to sit down and read a book? Look no further than audiobooks! With the rise of audible tech books, you can now learn about the latest technological advancements while on-the-go or during your downtime. In this blog post, we will discuss the best audible tech books to listen to in 2021.

As technology continues to evolve at a rapid pace, it can be challenging to keep up with the latest trends and advancements. Reading tech books can be time-consuming, and not everyone has the time to sit down and read. This is where audible tech books come in handy. They allow you to learn about the latest technological advancements while doing other things like driving, cleaning, or exercising.

The target of best audible tech books is to provide listeners with the latest technological advancements and trends without having to read books. These audiobooks are perfect for people who want to stay up-to-date with technology but don't have the time to read. They cover a wide range of topics, from coding to cybersecurity, and are narrated by experts in the field.

1. "The Innovators" by Walter Isaacson

As the author of the best-selling biography of Steve Jobs, Walter Isaacson brings his expertise on the topic of innovation to this audiobook. "The Innovators" tells the story of the people who created the digital revolution and how their inventions have changed the world. Isaacson's compelling storytelling makes this audiobook a must-listen for anyone interested in the history of technology.

2. "The Phoenix Project" by Gene Kim, Kevin Behr, and George Spafford

For those interested in the world of DevOps and IT, "The Phoenix Project" is the perfect audiobook. This novel centers around a fictional company's transformation journey from a chaotic and dysfunctional IT organization to one that runs smoothly and efficiently. It's a great listen for anyone looking to improve their IT and DevOps skills.

3. "The Code Book" by Simon Singh

If you're interested in cryptography and the history of codes, "The Code Book" is an audiobook you won't want to miss. Simon Singh takes listeners on a journey through the history of codes, from ancient Egypt to modern-day encryption. This audiobook is perfect for anyone interested in the history of codes and the role they played in shaping our world.

4. "The Art of Invisibility" by Kevin Mitnick

Kevin Mitnick is a former hacker turned security consultant. In "The Art of Invisibility," he shares his expertise on how to protect your digital privacy and identity. This audiobook covers a range of topics, from online tracking to password security, and is a must-listen for anyone concerned about their online privacy.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these audiobooks suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, these audiobooks are suitable for beginners. They are narrated in a way that is easy to understand and cover a range of topics for both beginners and experts.

Q: Can I listen to these audiobooks on any device?

A: Yes, you can listen to these audiobooks on any device that supports audiobooks, including smartphones, tablets, and computers.

Q: Are these audiobooks expensive?

A: The cost of these audiobooks varies, but they are generally priced similarly to their physical book counterparts. Audible also offers a monthly subscription service that allows you to listen to audiobooks for a lower cost.

Q: Can I listen to these audiobooks offline?

A: Yes, you can download these audiobooks for offline listening. This is perfect for those who want to listen to audiobooks while on-the-go without using their mobile data.

Conclusion of Best Audible Tech Books

As technology continues to advance, it's essential to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and advancements. Audiobooks are a great way to learn about technology while on-the-go or during your downtime. The four audible tech books mentioned in this blog post cover a range of topics and are perfect for both beginners and experts. Start listening today and stay ahead of the game!
