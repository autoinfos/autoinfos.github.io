Arizona Automotive Institute

The Arizona Automotive Institute, located in Glendale, Arizona, is one of the top automotive schools in Arizona. It offers a variety of automotive training programs, including Automotive Service Technology, Diesel-Heavy Truck, and High Performance Technology. The school has experienced instructors and state-of-the-art facilities that provide hands-on training to ensure students are prepared for the workforce upon graduation.

Universal Technical Institute

The Universal Technical Institute, located in Avondale, Arizona, is another top automotive school in Arizona. It offers a variety of automotive training programs, including Automotive Technology, Diesel Technology, and Collision Repair Technology. The school has a reputation for providing quality education and job placement assistance, making it a top choice for students looking to start their careers in the automotive industry.

Gateway Community College Automotive Technology Program

The Gateway Community College Automotive Technology Program, located in Phoenix, Arizona, offers a comprehensive automotive training program that includes classroom instruction and hands-on training. The program covers everything from basic automotive repair to advanced diagnostics and technology. The school has experienced instructors and state-of-the-art facilities that provide students with the skills they need to succeed in the automotive industry.
