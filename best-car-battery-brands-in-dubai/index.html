Question and Answer

Q: What size battery do I need for my car?

A: The size of the battery you need will depend on the make and model of your car. You can consult your owner's manual or speak with a mechanic to determine the correct size.

Q: How often should I replace my car battery?

A: Car batteries typically last between 3-5 years. It's a good idea to have your battery tested regularly and replace it if it's showing signs of wear or age.

Q: How do I know if my car battery is failing?

A: Signs of a failing car battery include difficulty starting the engine, dimming headlights, and unusual sounds or smells coming from the battery.

Q: Can I replace my car battery myself?

A: While it is possible to replace your car battery yourself, it's recommended to have a mechanic perform the replacement to ensure that it is installed correctly.
