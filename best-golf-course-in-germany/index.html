The Best Golf Course In Germany For A Relaxing Game

Written by Daniel Nov 10, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of crowded and noisy golf courses? Do you want to enjoy a relaxing game in a serene environment? If so, you should consider playing golf in Germany, home to some of the best golf courses in Europe. In this post, we will explore the best golf course in Germany and why it is perfect for a calming game.

If you are a golf enthusiast, you know how frustrating it can be to play on a crowded course. It is not only distracting but also affects your concentration and performance. Additionally, noisy environments can be stressful, making it difficult to enjoy the game. Fortunately, there is a solution to these problems: playing golf in a tranquil environment.

The best golf course in Germany for a relaxing game is the Bad Saarow Golf Resort. Located in the heart of Scharmützelsee, this golf course offers a serene environment that is perfect for a calming game. The resort features two 18-hole championship courses that are set amidst the picturesque landscape of Brandenburg.

In summary, if you want to enjoy a relaxing game of golf in Germany, the Bad Saarow Golf Resort is the place to go. With its tranquil environment and two championship courses, you are guaranteed to have a calming and enjoyable experience.

Why is the Bad Saarow Golf Resort the Best Golf Course in Germany?

As a golf enthusiast, I have played on several golf courses in Germany, but none compares to the Bad Saarow Golf Resort. The resort offers a unique combination of a serene environment and world-class facilities that make it the perfect place for a relaxing game of golf.

The resort features two championship courses that are designed to challenge and excite golfers of all skill levels. The Faldo Course, designed by six-time major winner Sir Nick Faldo, is a par-72 course that spans over 7,000 yards. The course features strategic bunkering, water hazards, and undulating greens that require precise shots and strategic thinking.

The Arnold Palmer Course, designed by the legendary Arnold Palmer, is a par-72 course that spans over 6,800 yards. The course features natural water hazards, elevated greens, and strategically placed bunkers that test golfers' skills and precision.

What Makes the Bad Saarow Golf Resort Unique?

Aside from its world-class facilities, the Bad Saarow Golf Resort is unique for its serene environment. The resort is located in the heart of Scharmützelsee, a picturesque landscape that is known for its tranquility and natural beauty. The resort's courses are set amidst this landscape, offering golfers a calming and relaxing experience that is hard to find in other golf courses.

Moreover, the resort offers several amenities that cater to golfers' needs, including a driving range, chipping and putting greens, and a pro shop. The resort also has a clubhouse that features a restaurant and bar, where golfers can relax and unwind after a game.

What are the Best Times to Play Golf at the Bad Saarow Golf Resort?

The best times to play golf at the Bad Saarow Golf Resort are during the spring and fall seasons. During these seasons, the weather is mild, and the courses are less crowded, allowing golfers to enjoy a calming and uninterrupted game. Additionally, the resort offers several packages and deals during these seasons, making it more affordable for golfers.

How to Get to the Bad Saarow Golf Resort?

The Bad Saarow Golf Resort is located in Brandenburg, Germany, about 65 kilometers southeast of Berlin. The easiest way to get to the resort is to drive from Berlin, which takes approximately 1 hour. Alternatively, you can take a train from Berlin to Bad Saarow, which takes approximately 1 hour and 15 minutes. From the train station, you can take a taxi to the resort.

My Personal Experience at the Bad Saarow Golf Resort

I visited the Bad Saarow Golf Resort last fall, and I was blown away by its beauty and serenity. The resort's natural landscape and world-class facilities make it the perfect place for a relaxing game of golf. I played on the Faldo Course, which was challenging but also exciting. The course's strategic bunkering and undulating greens required precise shots and strategic thinking, making the game more enjoyable. After the game, I relaxed at the resort's clubhouse, which offered a stunning view of the landscape and delicious food.

A: Yes, the resort offers several accommodation options, including rooms, suites, and apartments.

Conclusion of the Best Golf Course in Germany

The Bad Saarow Golf Resort is the best golf course in Germany for a relaxing game. With its serene environment, world-class facilities, and championship courses, the resort offers a unique and unforgettable experience for golfers of all levels. If you want to enjoy a calming and enjoyable game of golf, you should definitely visit the Bad Saarow Golf Resort.
