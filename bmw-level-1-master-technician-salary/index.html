Are you considering a career as a BMW Level 1 Master Technician? If so, you may be wondering how much you can expect to earn in this position. As a BMW Level 1 Master Technician, you'll be responsible for performing a wide range of diagnostic and repair services on BMW vehicles. This can include everything from routine maintenance tasks to more complex repairs, and it requires a high level of skill and expertise.
