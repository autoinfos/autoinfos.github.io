The Role of Experience in CAD Drafter Salary in Australia

Experience is a critical factor that affects CAD drafter salary in Australia. Entry-level CAD drafters with less than three years of experience can expect to earn around $50,000 to $60,000 per year. The Role of Location in CAD Drafter Salary in Australia

The location of employment is also a significant factor that affects CAD drafter salary in Australia. CAD drafters working in metropolitan areas such as Sydney and Melbourne tend to earn more than those working in regional areas.

Personal experience: When I worked as a CAD drafter in Sydney, my salary was around $75,000 per year. However, when I moved to a regional area to work as a CAD drafter, my salary decreased to $62,000 per year.

This is because the cost of living in metropolitan areas is higher than in regional areas. Therefore, employers in metropolitan areas tend to offer higher salaries to attract and retain skilled CAD drafters.
