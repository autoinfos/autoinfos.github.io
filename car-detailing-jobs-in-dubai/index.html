What Are the Different Types of Car Detailing Services?

There are several types of car detailing services available in Dubai, including:

  • Exterior Detailing: This involves cleaning and restoring the exterior of the car, including the paint, wheels, and tires.
  • Interior Detailing: This involves cleaning and conditioning the interior of the car, including the seats, carpets, and dashboard.
  • Full Detailing: This involves both exterior and interior detailing, as well as engine cleaning and restoration.

As a car detailer in Dubai, it is important to be familiar with all of these services and to be able to provide them to customers.
