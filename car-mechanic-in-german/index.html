Kraftstoffpumpe (Fuel Pump)

The fuel pump is a crucial part of your car's engine. It pumps fuel from the gas tank to the engine, where it's burned to power the car. If your fuel pump isn't working properly, your car won't be able to run. It's important to recognize signs of a failing fuel pump, such as difficulty starting your car, reduced fuel efficiency, and engine misfires.
