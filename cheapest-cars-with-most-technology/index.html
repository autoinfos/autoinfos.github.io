The Chevrolet Spark is the perfect car for those who are looking for a budget-friendly option that comes with the latest technology. This car is known for its impressive infotainment system that includes a 7-inch touchscreen display, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto compatibility. I personally own a Chevrolet Spark, and I can vouch for its reliable performance and excellent gas mileage.

Kia Rio

The Kia Rio is another car that offers the latest technology features at a reasonable price. This car comes with an 8-inch touchscreen display, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto compatibility. The interior of the car is spacious and provides a comfortable ride. I recently test drove a Kia Rio, and I was impressed by its smooth handling and sleek design.

Hyundai Accent

The Hyundai Accent is a popular car that offers advanced technology features at an affordable price. This car comes with a 7-inch touchscreen display, Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto compatibility. The car's interior is designed to provide a comfortable and spacious ride. I have heard positive reviews from friends who own a Hyundai Accent and praise its reliability and fuel efficiency.
