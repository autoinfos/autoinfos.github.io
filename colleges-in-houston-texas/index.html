Discovering Houston Community College

Houston Community College is another great option for students looking to pursue careers in interior design. The school offers a variety of programs at different levels, including an Associate of Applied Science in Interior Design and a Certificate of Completion in Interior Design.

One of the advantages of attending Houston Community College is its affordability. The campus is also conveniently located in the heart of Houston, making it easy to access from anywhere in the city.

Understanding the University of Houston

The University of Houston is one of the largest and most diverse universities in Houston, offering a wide range of programs in many different fields. While the school is not specifically focused on interior design, it does offer programs in related fields such as architecture and art.
