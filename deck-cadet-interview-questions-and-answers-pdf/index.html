What is the role of a deck cadet?

A deck cadet is an entry-level position in the maritime industry. The primary role of a deck cadet is to assist the deck officers in the maintenance and operation of the vessel. They are responsible for the upkeep of the deck and the safety of the crew and passengers. A deck cadet is also responsible for navigation, cargo handling, and communication.
