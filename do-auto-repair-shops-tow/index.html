What to Expect When Auto Repair Shops Tow

When an auto repair shop tows your car, they will usually charge you for the service. The cost can vary depending on the distance they need to tow your car and the type of vehicle you have. How to Prepare for Auto Repair Shop Towing

If you know that you'll need to have your car towed to a repair shop, there are a few things you can do to prepare. First, make sure that you have all of your personal belongings out of the car. You don't want to leave anything valuable behind.

You should also remove any important documents from the car, such as your registration and insurance information. Keep these documents with you so that you can provide them to the repair shop when you arrive.
