If you have a suspended license, it can be difficult to rent a car. Rental car companies have strict policies when it comes to driver's licenses, and they will check to make sure that your license is valid before allowing you to rent a car. This can be a pain point for many people, especially those who have had their license suspended for a minor infraction.

The short answer to the question of whether rental car companies check if your license is suspended is yes. Rental car companies are required by law to check your driver's license before renting a car to you. This is to ensure that you are legally allowed to drive and have a valid driver's license.

What Happens if Your License is Suspended?

If your license is suspended, you will not be able to rent a car from a rental car company. Rental car companies are legally required to check your driver's license before renting you a car, and if your license is suspended, they will not be able to rent you a car. In addition, driving with a suspended license is illegal and can result in fines, jail time, and other legal consequences.

How to Check if Your License is Suspended

If you're not sure if your license is valid, you can check with your local Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV). Most DMVs have an online system where you can check the status of your license. You can also call your local DMV or visit in person to check the status of your license.

What to Do if Your License is Suspended

If your license is suspended, you'll need to take steps to get it reinstated. This may involve paying fines, completing a driver improvement course, or other requirements. Once your license is reinstated, you'll be able to rent a car from a rental car company.
