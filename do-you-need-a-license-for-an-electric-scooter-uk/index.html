Do You Need A License For An Electric Scooter In The Uk

Written by Aprilia Aug 17, 2023 · 4 min read

Electric scooters have become a popular mode of transportation in the UK, especially in urban areas. They are convenient, eco-friendly, and cost-effective. However, before purchasing an electric scooter, many people wonder if they need a license to ride one in the UK. In this blog post, we will explore the answer to this question and everything else you need to know about electric scooter licenses in the UK.

Many people are confused about the legal requirements for riding an electric scooter in the UK. The lack of clarity can make it difficult to know what you need to do to stay safe and legal. Some of the pain points related to this topic include confusion about age restrictions, insurance requirements, and the need for a driving license.

The short answer to the question is that, currently, you do need a license to ride an electric scooter in the UK. However, the rules are changing, and trials are underway in many areas of the country to test the viability of electric scooters on UK roads. During these trials, electric scooters are allowed on the road, and riders do not need a license.

In summary, the rules around electric scooters and licenses in the UK are changing. Currently, you do need a license to ride an electric scooter, but trials are underway to test the feasibility of allowing e-scooters on UK roads.

Do You Need a License for an Electric Scooter in the UK?

As mentioned earlier, currently, you do need a license to ride an electric scooter in the UK. However, this is set to change, and trials are underway in many areas of the country to test the feasibility of allowing e-scooters on UK roads. During these trials, electric scooters are allowed on the road, and riders do not need a license.

When I first got my electric scooter, I was unsure about whether I needed a license or not. I did some research and found out that, at the time, you did need a license. However, I also learned that trials were taking place in some parts of the country, and I kept an eye out for any updates in my area.

Age Restrictions and Other Requirements

If you are planning to ride an electric scooter in the UK, there are a few other requirements you need to be aware of, aside from needing a license. Currently, you must be at least 16 years old to ride an electric scooter, and the scooter must meet certain safety standards. Additionally, you must wear a helmet when riding an electric scooter on a public road.

Insurance Requirements

If you are riding an electric scooter during the trial period, you do not need insurance. However, if you plan to ride an electric scooter after the trials end, you will need insurance. It is important to note that not all insurance providers offer electric scooter insurance, so you will need to do some research to find a provider that does.

How to Get a License for an Electric Scooter in the UK

Currently, you can obtain a license for an electric scooter in the UK by taking a CBT (Compulsory Basic Training) course. This course is designed for people who want to ride a motorcycle or moped, but it also applies to electric scooters. Once you have completed the course, you will receive a certificate that allows you to ride an electric scooter legally on UK roads.

Conclusion of Do You Need a License for an Electric Scooter in the UK

In conclusion, the rules around electric scooters and licenses in the UK are changing. Currently, you do need a license to ride an electric scooter, but trials are underway to test the feasibility of allowing e-scooters on UK roads. If you are planning to ride an electric scooter, it is important to be aware of the legal requirements and to stay up-to-date with any changes in the law.
