Do You Need a License to Be a Mechanic in California?

Yes, in most cases you do need a license to be a mechanic in California. This is because the state requires certain types of automotive professionals to be licensed in order to protect consumers from unscrupulous or incompetent operators. Specifically, if you plan to work as an auto repair technician, brake adjuster, or smog technician in California, you will need to obtain a license from the Bureau of Automotive Repair (BAR).
