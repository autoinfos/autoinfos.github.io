And when it comes to the automotive industry, there are often mixed opinions on whether companies pay their employees fairly. So, does Carmax pay well? The answer is yes, Carmax is known for offering competitive salaries to its employees. According to Glassdoor, the average salary for a Carmax sales associate is $47,000 per year, with some sales associates earning upwards of $85,000 per year. In summary, Carmax does pay well compared to other car dealerships in the industry. They offer competitive salaries and benefits that make them a desirable employer. However, it's important to note that salaries may vary depending on location, job position, and experience level.

Carmax determines salaries based on several factors, including job position, location, education level, and experience.
