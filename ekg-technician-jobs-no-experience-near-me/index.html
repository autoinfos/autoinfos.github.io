What is an EKG Technician?

An EKG technician is a healthcare professional who performs electrocardiogram (EKG) tests to help diagnose and treat heart conditions. EKG technicians work in a variety of settings, including hospitals, clinics, and physician offices. They are responsible for setting up and operating EKG machines, preparing patients for EKG tests, and recording and analyzing test results.
