What Are Entry Level Auto Body Jobs?

Entry level auto body jobs are exactly what they sound like: positions within the auto body industry that require little to no prior experience. These jobs typically involve tasks like sanding, painting, and prepping vehicles for repairs. While they may not be glamorous, they offer valuable experience and can lead to more advanced positions down the line.
