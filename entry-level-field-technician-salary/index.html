Entry Level Field Technician Salary What You Need To Know

Written by Frank Sep 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you considering a career as an entry level field technician? If so, you may be wondering how much you can expect to earn. The good news is that this is a growing field with plenty of opportunities, but it's important to understand the salary expectations before diving in.

One of the biggest pain points when it comes to entry level field technician salary is the potential for low pay. Many people worry that they won't be able to make ends meet if they start out in this field. However, it's important to keep in mind that this is an entry level position, and pay will increase as you gain more experience and skills.

So, what can you expect in terms of salary as an entry level field technician? According to data from Glassdoor, the average base pay for this position is around $38,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on factors such as location, industry, and specific job duties.

In summary, entry level field technician salary can be a concern for those considering this career path. However, it's important to remember that this is just a starting point and pay will increase with time and experience.

What is Entry Level Field Technician Salary?

Entry level field technician salary refers to the pay that individuals at the beginning of their career in this field can expect to earn. This is typically a starting point, with pay increases coming as the individual gains more experience and skills.

Personally, I started out as an entry level field technician and earned around $35,000 per year. It wasn't a huge salary, but it was enough to cover my basic expenses and allowed me to gain valuable experience in the field.

Factors That Affect Entry Level Field Technician Salary

As mentioned earlier, there are several factors that can impact entry level field technician salary. One of the biggest is location. For example, someone working in a major city may earn more than someone working in a rural area.

Industry can also play a role in salary expectations. For example, someone working in the telecommunications industry may earn more than someone working in the HVAC industry.

Skills and Experience

Of course, skills and experience are also major factors when it comes to entry level field technician salary. Someone with more experience and skills may be able to command a higher salary than someone who is just starting out.

How to Increase Your Entry Level Field Technician Salary

If you're looking to increase your entry level field technician salary, there are several steps you can take. One is to continue learning and developing new skills. This can make you more valuable to employers and may lead to higher pay.

You can also consider seeking out certifications or other credentials that demonstrate your expertise in the field. This can help you stand out from other candidates and may lead to higher pay.

Final Thoughts on Entry Level Field Technician Salary

While entry level field technician salary may be a concern for some, it's important to remember that this is just a starting point. With time, experience, and continued learning, you can increase your earning potential and build a successful career in this field.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average entry level field technician salary?

A: According to Glassdoor, the average base pay for this position is around $38,000 per year.

Q: What factors can impact entry level field technician salary?

A: Location, industry, skills, and experience can all impact entry level field technician salary.

Q: Is it possible to increase entry level field technician salary?

A: Yes, by continuing to learn and develop new skills, seeking out certifications, and gaining experience, it's possible to increase entry level field technician salary.

Q: Is entry level field technician a good career choice?

A: Yes, entry level field technician can be a great career choice for those interested in the field. It offers room for growth and plenty of opportunities.

Conclusion of Entry Level Field Technician Salary

Entry level field technician salary may be a concern for some, but it's important to remember that this is just the beginning. With hard work, dedication, and continued learning, you can increase your earning potential and build a successful career in this field.
