What is Facility Maintenance Engineering?

Facility maintenance engineering is a field that involves the management, repair, and maintenance of buildings, equipment, and systems within an organization. This field plays a critical role in ensuring that facilities are operating safely, efficiently, and effectively. Facility maintenance engineers are responsible for designing, implementing, and overseeing maintenance programs, as well as troubleshooting and resolving any issues that arise.
