Answering the Target of Heavy Equipment Operator Salary Alberta

So, what can you expect in terms of salary as a heavy equipment operator in Alberta? According to Payscale, the average hourly wage for heavy equipment operators in Alberta is CA$29.28. However, this can range from CA$19 to CA$44 per hour, depending on the factors mentioned earlier.
