As a mechanical engineer, your main role is to design, develop, and test mechanical devices and systems. Aircraft Maintenance: A High-Stakes Career

As an aircraft mechanic, your main role is to inspect, repair, and maintain aircraft. This job requires attention to detail, technical knowledge, and a commitment to safety. I have worked as an aircraft mechanic for over 7 years, and I can say that this job is both challenging and exciting.
