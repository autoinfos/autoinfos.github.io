Highest Paying Engineering Jobs In Egypt Everything You Need To Know

Written by Luffy Jun 03, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you an engineering graduate looking for the highest paying engineering jobs in Egypt? Do you want to know which engineering fields are most in-demand in Egypt and which companies offer the best salaries? If so, then this blog post is for you.

Engineering is one of the most sought-after professions in Egypt, and for good reason. Engineers are responsible for designing, building, and maintaining the infrastructure that keeps our society functioning. However, finding the right engineering job can be a challenge, especially if you're looking for a high-paying position in a competitive field.

If you're looking for the highest paying engineering jobs in Egypt, you need to focus on the most in-demand fields. These include civil engineering, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, chemical engineering, and petroleum engineering. These fields offer some of the best salaries in Egypt, with experienced engineers earning up to 20,000 EGP per month or more.

In this blog post, we'll take a closer look at the highest paying engineering jobs in Egypt, the companies that offer the best salaries, and the skills and qualifications you need to succeed in these fields.

Civil Engineering

Civil engineering is one of the oldest and most respected engineering fields in Egypt. Civil engineers design and build infrastructure such as roads, bridges, buildings, and water supply systems. They also oversee construction projects and ensure that they are completed on time and within budget.

As a civil engineer in Egypt, you can expect to earn an average salary of around 10,000 EGP per month. However, experienced civil engineers can earn up to 20,000 EGP per month or more.

To succeed as a civil engineer in Egypt, you need to have a strong understanding of structural engineering, construction methods, and project management. You also need to have excellent communication and leadership skills, as you'll be working with a team of engineers and construction workers.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering is another in-demand field in Egypt. Mechanical engineers design and build machines, engines, and other mechanical systems. They also oversee manufacturing processes and ensure that products are built to high standards of quality.

In Egypt, experienced mechanical engineers can earn up to 20,000 EGP per month or more. However, the average salary for a mechanical engineer in Egypt is around 8,000 EGP per month.

To succeed as a mechanical engineer in Egypt, you need to have a strong understanding of mechanics, thermodynamics, and materials science. You also need to be able to work well in a team and have excellent problem-solving skills.

Electrical Engineering

Electrical engineering is a highly specialized field that involves designing and building electrical systems and equipment. Electrical engineers work on everything from power generation and transmission to consumer electronics and telecommunications.

In Egypt, experienced electrical engineers can earn up to 18,000 EGP per month or more. However, the average salary for an electrical engineer in Egypt is around 10,000 EGP per month.

To succeed as an electrical engineer in Egypt, you need to have a strong understanding of electrical theory, circuit design, and computer-aided design (CAD) software. You also need to be able to work well in a team and have excellent communication skills.

Chemical Engineering

Chemical engineering is a highly specialized field that involves designing and developing chemical processes and equipment. Chemical engineers work in a variety of industries, including pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and energy.

In Egypt, experienced chemical engineers can earn up to 20,000 EGP per month or more. However, the average salary for a chemical engineer in Egypt is around 12,000 EGP per month.

To succeed as a chemical engineer in Egypt, you need to have a strong understanding of chemistry, thermodynamics, and process design. You also need to be able to work well in a team and have excellent problem-solving skills.

Petroleum Engineering

Petroleum engineering is one of the highest paying engineering fields in Egypt. Petroleum engineers design and develop methods for extracting oil and gas from the earth. They also oversee drilling operations and ensure that they are done safely and efficiently.

In Egypt, experienced petroleum engineers can earn up to 30,000 EGP per month or more. However, the average salary for a petroleum engineer in Egypt is around 18,000 EGP per month.

To succeed as a petroleum engineer in Egypt, you need to have a strong understanding of geology, drilling techniques, and reservoir engineering. You also need to be able to work well in a team and have excellent communication skills.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average salary for an engineer in Egypt?

A: The average salary for an engineer in Egypt depends on the field of engineering. Civil engineers earn an average of 10,000 EGP per month, while petroleum engineers can earn up to 30,000 EGP per month or more.

Q: Which engineering field is most in-demand in Egypt?

A: The most in-demand engineering fields in Egypt are civil engineering, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, chemical engineering, and petroleum engineering.

Q: What skills do I need to succeed as an engineer in Egypt?

A: To succeed as an engineer in Egypt, you need to have a strong understanding of your field of engineering, as well as excellent communication skills, problem-solving skills, and leadership skills.

Q: Which companies offer the best salaries for engineers in Egypt?

A: Some of the companies that offer the best salaries for engineers in Egypt include ExxonMobil, Shell, BP, and Schlumberger.

Conclusion of Highest Paying Engineering Jobs in Egypt

If you're an engineering graduate looking for the highest paying engineering jobs in Egypt, then you need to focus on the most in-demand fields. These include civil engineering, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, chemical engineering, and petroleum engineering. By developing the right skills and qualifications, you can position yourself for success in these fields and earn a high salary in Egypt's competitive job market.
