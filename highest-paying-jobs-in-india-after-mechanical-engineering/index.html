First and foremost, one of the highest paying jobs in India after mechanical engineering is a technical consultant. Another high-paying job option is a research and development engineer. In this role, you will be responsible for developing new products and technologies. This job requires creativity and innovative thinking, as well as a deep understanding of mechanical engineering principles.
