Requirements for Becoming a Xray Tech in Texas

Along with completing an accredited program, there are additional requirements for becoming a xray tech in Texas. These may include passing a background check and drug screening, as well as meeting certain physical requirements, such as being able to lift and move patients.

Additionally, you will need to pass the ARRT exam in order to become certified. This exam tests your knowledge and skills in radiologic technology and is required by most states, including Texas.

Program Options for Becoming a Xray Tech in Texas

There are several program options for those interested in becoming a xray tech in Texas. These may include traditional two-year programs, as well as shorter certificate programs. Some schools also offer online programs, which can be a convenient option for those with busy schedules.

When choosing a program, it is important to ensure that it is accredited by the Joint Review Committee on Education in Radiologic Technology (JRCERT). This will ensure that the program meets the necessary standards for education and training in radiologic technology.

How to Prepare for a Career as a Xray Tech in Texas
