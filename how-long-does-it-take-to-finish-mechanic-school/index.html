Types of Mechanic School Programs

There are various types of mechanic school programs available. Here are some of the most popular ones:

Certificate or Diploma Programs

Certificate or diploma programs are the most common type of mechanic school program. They typically take six months to two years to complete, and the curriculum covers all aspects of automotive repair and maintenance. Certificate or diploma programs tend to focus more on hands-on training, and students can get practical experience working on real cars.

Associate Degree Programs

Associate degree programs are more comprehensive and take up to four years to complete. These programs cover all aspects of automotive repair and maintenance, but they also include general education courses. Associate degree programs tend to focus more on theory than hands-on training, and students can get a deeper understanding of the automotive industry.
