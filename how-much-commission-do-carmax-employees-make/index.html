How Much Commission Do Carmax Employees Make

Written by Luffy Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career at Carmax? One of the most common questions asked by potential employees is how much commission do Carmax employees make? The commission structure can play a crucial role in determining whether or not Carmax is the right fit for you.

At Carmax, employees earn a commission based on the gross profit of the car they sell. This means that the more expensive the car, the higher the commission. However, the commission percentage varies based on the location of the dealership, the employee's experience, and their job title.

The commission percentage for sales consultants ranges from 4% to 8% of the gross profit, with an average of around 6%. Meanwhile, sales managers have a commission range of 1% to 2.5% of the dealership's gross profit, with an average of around 2%. Keep in mind that these percentages are subject to change and may vary based on the dealership.

In conclusion, Carmax employees earn a commission based on the gross profit of the car they sell, with the commission percentage varying based on location, experience, and job title. Sales consultants have an average commission of around 6%, while sales managers have an average commission of around 2%.

How is the Commission Calculated?

When I worked as a sales consultant at Carmax, my commission was calculated by multiplying the gross profit of the car by the commission percentage. For example, if I sold a car with a gross profit of $2,000 and my commission percentage was 6%, my commission would be $120. It's essential to note that the commission percentage changes based on the dealership and is not fixed.

How to Maximize Your Commission at Carmax?

The key to maximizing your commission at Carmax is to sell more expensive cars. The higher the gross profit of the car you sell, the higher your commission percentage will be. Additionally, building strong relationships with customers and providing excellent customer service can lead to repeat business and positive reviews, both of which can increase your sales and commission.

Do Carmax Employees Receive Any Additional Benefits?

Yes, Carmax offers its employees several benefits, including health insurance, dental and vision coverage, retirement plans, and paid time off. Additionally, there are opportunities for career advancement within the company, with many of the sales consultants moving up to management positions.

Are There Any Downsides to Working at Carmax?

One of the main downsides of working at Carmax is the lack of flexibility in the work schedule. As a sales consultant, you are required to work weekends and holidays, which can be a challenge for those with family commitments or other obligations on weekends. Additionally, the commission structure can be unpredictable, with slow sales periods resulting in lower paychecks.

Conclusion of How Much Commission do Carmax Employees Make

Overall, Carmax offers its employees a competitive commission structure based on the gross profit of the car sold. While the commission percentage varies based on location, experience, and job title, sales consultants have an average commission of around 6%, while sales managers have an average commission of around 2%. Maximizing your commission at Carmax involves selling more expensive cars, building strong relationships with customers, and providing excellent customer service. While there are some downsides to working at Carmax, such as the lack of schedule flexibility, the benefits and opportunities for career advancement make it an attractive option for many job seekers.

Question and Answer

Q: How does the commission structure at Carmax compare to other dealerships?
A: The commission structure at Carmax is generally considered competitive compared to other dealerships. However, the commission percentage varies based on location and job title.

Q: Can sales consultants negotiate their commission percentage?
A: No, the commission percentage is non-negotiable and determined by the dealership.

Q: How often do Carmax employees receive their commission checks?
A: Carmax employees receive their commission checks once a month, on the 15th of the following month.

Q: Is there a minimum amount of cars a sales consultant must sell to earn a commission?
A: No, there is no minimum amount of cars a sales consultant must sell to earn a commission. However, the more cars sold, the higher the commission.
