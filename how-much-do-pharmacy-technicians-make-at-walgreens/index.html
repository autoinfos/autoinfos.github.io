How Much Do Pharmacy Technicians Make At Walgreens

Written by Oliver Aug 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Have you ever wondered how much pharmacy technicians make at Walgreens? Maybe you're considering a career as a pharmacy technician and want to know what kind of salary you can expect. Or maybe you're already working as a pharmacy technician and want to compare your salary to what others are making. Whatever the reason, it's important to have a clear understanding of the earning potential for pharmacy technicians at Walgreens.

Pharmacy technicians play an important role in the healthcare industry. They work alongside pharmacists to fill prescriptions, manage inventory, and provide customer service. However, the job can be demanding, with long hours on your feet and a high level of responsibility. As such, it's important to be fairly compensated for your work.

So, how much do pharmacy technicians make at Walgreens? According to Glassdoor, the average hourly rate for a pharmacy technician at Walgreens is $13. Hourly rates can range from $9 to $19, depending on factors such as experience, location, and job performance. Additionally, pharmacy technicians at Walgreens may be eligible for benefits such as health insurance, paid time off, and 401(k) plans.

In summary, pharmacy technicians at Walgreens can expect to earn an average hourly rate of $13, with the potential for higher pay based on experience and performance. Additionally, benefits such as health insurance and paid time off may be available.

What Determines a Pharmacy Technician's Salary at Walgreens?

As mentioned earlier, there are several factors that can impact a pharmacy technician's salary at Walgreens. One of the biggest factors is experience. Pharmacy technicians with more experience are often able to command higher salaries, as they bring a greater level of expertise to the job. Additionally, pharmacy technicians who perform well on the job and receive positive feedback from customers and supervisors may be eligible for raises or bonuses.

My Experience as a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens

As a former pharmacy technician at Walgreens, I can attest to the fact that the job can be demanding but rewarding. While the pay may not be as high as some other healthcare positions, the ability to help people and make a difference in their lives is incredibly fulfilling. Additionally, the opportunity to work alongside pharmacists and other healthcare professionals can be a great learning experience.

Other Factors to Consider When Evaluating Pharmacy Technician Salaries

When evaluating pharmacy technician salaries, it's important to consider factors beyond just hourly pay. For example, some employers may offer benefits such as health insurance, paid time off, and retirement plans. Additionally, some employers may offer opportunities for advancement, such as the ability to become a lead pharmacy technician or move into a management role.

How to Maximize Your Salary as a Pharmacy Technician at Walgreens

If you're looking to maximize your salary as a pharmacy technician at Walgreens, there are several things you can do. First and foremost, focus on providing excellent customer service and performing your job to the best of your abilities. This can help you receive positive feedback from customers and supervisors, which can lead to raises and bonuses. Additionally, consider pursuing additional education or training, such as becoming certified through the Pharmacy Technician Certification Board. This can help you stand out from other pharmacy technicians and command a higher salary.

Conclusion of How Much Do Pharmacy Technicians Make at Walgreens

While the average hourly rate for a pharmacy technician at Walgreens is $13, there are several factors that can impact a technician's salary, including experience, location, and job performance. Additionally, pharmacy technicians may be eligible for benefits such as health insurance and paid time off. By focusing on providing excellent customer service and pursuing additional education or training, pharmacy technicians can maximize their earning potential at Walgreens.
