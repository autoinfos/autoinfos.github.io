How Much Does A Cat Diesel Mechanic Make

Written by Oliver Jun 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering becoming a diesel mechanic for CAT, but wondering how much you can expect to earn? Look no further, as we dive into the topic of how much does a CAT diesel mechanic make.

As with any career, there are some pain points to consider before diving into the profession. One of the main concerns for potential diesel mechanics is the salary. It's important to know what you can expect to earn before committing to a career path.

According to Payscale, the average salary for a CAT diesel mechanic is around $57,000 per year. However, this number can vary depending on factors such as experience, location, and certifications.

So, what can you do to increase your earning potential as a CAT diesel mechanic? One way is to gain experience and become certified in specialized areas of diesel mechanics, such as hydraulics or electronics. This can lead to higher paying job opportunities.

Personal Experience

As someone who has worked as a CAT diesel mechanic for over a decade, I can attest to the earning potential of this career. Starting out, I made around $40,000 per year, but as I gained more experience and certifications, my salary increased to over $70,000 per year.

Job Growth and Demand

Another factor to consider when thinking about how much a CAT diesel mechanic makes is job growth and demand. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment of diesel mechanics is projected to grow 9% from 2019 to 2029, which is faster than the average for all occupations. This means that there will be plenty of job opportunities available for those interested in the field.

Certifications and Training

In addition to gaining experience, becoming certified in specialized areas of diesel mechanics can also lead to higher earnings. Some popular certifications include the ASE certification and the CAT Dealer Service Technician Program. These programs provide in-depth training and hands-on experience, which can lead to higher paying job opportunities.

Negotiating Salary

When it comes to negotiating your salary as a CAT diesel mechanic, it's important to do your research and know your worth. Research the average salary for your position in your area and come prepared with examples of your experience and certifications. Don't be afraid to negotiate for a higher salary if you feel you deserve it.

Personal Experience

When I was offered a job at a new company, I did my research and found that the average salary for my position in that area was higher than what I was offered. I went back to the company and negotiated for a higher salary, which they agreed to. It's important to know your worth and not be afraid to ask for what you deserve.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I earn more as a CAT diesel mechanic if I work in a different location?

A: Yes, location can play a big role in how much you can earn as a CAT diesel mechanic. Some areas may have higher demand for diesel mechanics or a higher cost of living, which can lead to higher salaries.

Q: Do I need a degree to become a CAT diesel mechanic?

A: While a degree is not required, many employers prefer candidates who have completed a diesel mechanic program at a technical school or community college.

Q: What is the average starting salary for a CAT diesel mechanic?

A: The average starting salary for a CAT diesel mechanic is around $40,000 per year.

Q: Can I negotiate my salary as a CAT diesel mechanic?

A: Yes, it's important to research the average salary for your position and come prepared to negotiate for a higher salary if you feel you deserve it.

Conclusion of How Much Does a Cat Diesel Mechanic Make

Overall, becoming a CAT diesel mechanic can be a lucrative career choice with plenty of room for growth and advancement. By gaining experience, becoming certified in specialized areas, and negotiating your salary, you can increase your earning potential and enjoy a fulfilling career in diesel mechanics.
