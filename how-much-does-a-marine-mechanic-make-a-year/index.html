Answer to How Much Does a Marine Mechanic Make a Year

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for marine mechanics and service technicians was $44,670 as of May 2020. The lowest 10 percent earned less than $25,820, while the highest 10 percent earned more than $76,410.
