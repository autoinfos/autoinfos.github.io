Are you interested in becoming a surgical technician in California but unsure about the salary range? It's essential to know how much you can expect to earn before jumping into a new career field. In this article, we'll provide you with all the information you need to know about how much a surgical technician makes in California.

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), the average surgical technician salary in California is $56,070 per year or $26.96 per hour. This salary is higher than the national average for surgical technicians, which is $50,110 per year or $24.08 per hour. However, the salary range for surgical technicians in California can vary depending on factors such as experience, education, location, and employer.

Location is another important factor that can affect a surgical technician's salary in California.
