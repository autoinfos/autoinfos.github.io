Are you considering working as a delivery driver for Advance Auto Parts but wondering about the pay? Do you want to know how much does Advance Auto Parts pay delivery drivers? Look no further, as we have all the information you need!

Working as a delivery driver can be a challenging job. You have to be on the road for hours, deal with traffic, and handle packages. However, the pay can make it worth it. A lot of people consider working as a delivery driver to make some extra money or as a full-time job.

According to Glassdoor, the average hourly pay for an Advance Auto Parts Delivery Driver is $13. This pay can vary depending on several factors such as location, experience, and job duties. Some delivery drivers may be paid a fixed salary, while others may be paid hourly.

In addition to the base pay, delivery drivers may also receive benefits such as health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off. These benefits can vary depending on the company's policies and the driver's employment status (full-time, part-time, or contract).

Factors That Affect Pay

As mentioned earlier, several factors can affect how much Advance Auto Parts pays its delivery drivers. One of the main factors is location. Drivers in urban areas may be paid more than those in rural areas due to the higher cost of living and increased demand.

Experience is another factor that can affect pay. Drivers with more experience may be paid more than those who are new to the job. Job duties can also affect pay, as drivers who have to handle heavy packages or work longer hours may be paid more.

Benefits of Working as a Delivery Driver for Advance Auto Parts

Aside from the competitive pay, working as a delivery driver for Advance Auto Parts can have several benefits. One of the main benefits is the flexible schedule. Drivers can choose to work full-time, part-time, or on a contract basis.

Another benefit is the opportunity for career growth. Drivers who perform well and show dedication to their job can be promoted to higher positions within the company.

  • Be physically fit and able to handle packages
