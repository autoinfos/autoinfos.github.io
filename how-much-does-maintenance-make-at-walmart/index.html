How Much Does Maintenance Make At Walmart

Written by Robby Sep 30, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Have you ever wondered how much maintenance workers make at Walmart? Perhaps you're considering a job in this field and want to know what kind of pay you can expect. Or maybe you're simply curious about the wages of Walmart employees. Whatever your reason for wanting to know, we've got you covered.

For some people, the idea of working in maintenance may not sound very appealing. After all, it can involve a lot of physical labor and cleaning up messes that others have left behind. However, for those who enjoy working with their hands and take pride in keeping things clean and functional, maintenance can be a rewarding and fulfilling career.

So, how much does maintenance make at Walmart? According to Glassdoor, the average hourly wage for a Walmart maintenance worker is $11. This can vary depending on a number of factors, such as location, experience, and job performance. Some maintenance workers at Walmart may make more than $11 per hour if they have specialized skills or work in a higher-paying area.

In summary, maintenance workers at Walmart can expect to make an average of $11 per hour. This is a decent wage for an entry-level position and can provide a good starting point for those looking to build a career in maintenance.

What Are the Job Duties of a Walmart Maintenance Worker?

As a former Walmart maintenance worker myself, I can tell you that the job involves a wide range of tasks. Some of the most common duties include cleaning and sanitizing the store, restocking shelves, and performing basic repairs. You may also be responsible for handling spills, maintaining the store's equipment, and assisting customers with various needs.

What Are the Pros and Cons of Working in Maintenance at Walmart?

One of the biggest advantages of working in maintenance at Walmart is the job security. This is a position that is always in demand, as stores need to be kept clean and functional at all times. Additionally, you may have opportunities for advancement within the company if you show a strong work ethic and willingness to learn.

However, there are also some downsides to consider. The job can be physically demanding and may require you to work long hours or odd shifts. You may also be exposed to various cleaning chemicals and other hazards, so it's important to take proper safety precautions.

How Can You Improve Your Chances of Getting Hired as a Walmart Maintenance Worker?

If you're interested in working in maintenance at Walmart, there are a few things you can do to increase your chances of getting hired. First, make sure you have a strong work history and can provide references from previous employers. Additionally, you may want to consider taking classes or earning certifications in areas such as HVAC, plumbing, or electrical work.

How Can You Negotiate a Higher Salary as a Walmart Maintenance Worker?

If you feel that you're not being paid fairly for your work as a maintenance worker at Walmart, there are a few steps you can take to negotiate a higher salary. First, make sure you have a clear understanding of your job duties and responsibilities, and be prepared to provide evidence of your skills and experience. Additionally, you may want to research the average salaries for maintenance workers in your area and use this information to make a case for a raise.

What Are Some Tips for Succeeding as a Walmart Maintenance Worker?

As someone who has worked in maintenance at Walmart, I can tell you that there are a few key things you can do to succeed in this role. First, always be willing to go above and beyond your job duties to help out your coworkers and ensure that the store is running smoothly. Additionally, make sure you're taking proper safety precautions and following all company policies and procedures. Finally, don't be afraid to ask for help or guidance when needed, as there is always something new to learn on the job.

Question and Answer Section:

Q: Is it hard to get hired as a Walmart maintenance worker?

A: It can depend on a variety of factors, such as the location and the number of available positions. However, having a strong work history and relevant skills can increase your chances of getting hired.

Q: What kind of benefits do Walmart maintenance workers receive?

A: This can vary depending on the location and the worker's status (full-time vs. part-time). However, some common benefits may include health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off.

Q: Are there opportunities for advancement as a Walmart maintenance worker?

A: Yes, there may be opportunities for advancement within the company if you show a strong work ethic and willingness to learn. Some maintenance workers may eventually become supervisors or move into other roles within the store.

Q: What kind of training is required to become a Walmart maintenance worker?

A: The specific training requirements may vary depending on the location and the worker's job duties. However, new hires will typically receive on-the-job training and may be required to complete additional training courses or certifications as needed.

Conclusion of How Much Does Maintenance Make at Walmart:

Working in maintenance at Walmart can be a good career choice for those who enjoy physical labor and take pride in keeping things clean and functional. While the average hourly wage for a Walmart maintenance worker is $11, there may be opportunities for higher pay depending on location and job performance. By taking proper safety precautions and showing a strong work ethic, you can succeed in this role and potentially advance within the company.
