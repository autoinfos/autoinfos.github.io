How Much Is Engineer Salary In The Philippines

Written by Luffy Aug 31, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering pursuing a career in engineering in the Philippines but wondering if it will pay off financially? You're not alone. Many aspiring engineers are curious about how much they can expect to earn in this field. In this blog post, we'll explore the average engineer salary in the Philippines and other important information that can help you make an informed decision about your future career.

As an engineer, you'll be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining structures, machines, and systems. While this field can be intellectually stimulating and rewarding, it's also known for being highly competitive and challenging. One of the biggest concerns for individuals considering a career in engineering is the salary. After all, you want to ensure that your hard work and dedication are compensated fairly.

According to data from PayScale, the average engineer salary in the Philippines is PHP 443,873 per year. However, this figure can vary significantly depending on your level of experience, specialization, and other factors. For instance, entry-level engineers typically earn between PHP 191,000 and PHP 468,000 per year, while senior engineers can earn as much as PHP 1,500,000 or more.

Personal Experience with Engineer Salary in the Philippines

As a graduate of Chemical Engineering in the Philippines, I started my career as a Process Engineer in a manufacturing company. My starting salary was PHP 20,000 per month or PHP 240,000 per year. After a few years of experience, I was able to negotiate a higher salary of PHP 50,000 per month or PHP 600,000 per year. As I gained more experience and took on more responsibilities, my salary continued to increase.

Factors that Affect Engineer Salary in the Philippines

Aside from experience and specialization, there are other factors that can affect how much you can earn as an engineer in the Philippines. For instance, the industry you work in can play a role in your salary. Engineers in certain industries, such as oil and gas or construction, tend to earn more than those in other industries. Your location can also affect your salary, with engineers in Metro Manila typically earning more than those in other parts of the country.

Specializations in Engineering and Their Salary Range

Specializing in a particular area of engineering can also impact your salary. Some specializations, such as petroleum engineering or software engineering, tend to pay higher salaries than others. Here are some of the average salaries for different engineering specializations in the Philippines:

  • Mechanical Engineer - PHP 312,000 per year
  • Civil Engineer - PHP 239,000 per year
  • Electrical Engineer - PHP 325,000 per year
  • Chemical Engineer - PHP 351,000 per year
  • Software Engineer - PHP 419,000 per year
  • Petroleum Engineer - PHP 1,200,000 per year

Tips for Negotiating Engineer Salary in the Philippines

If you're looking to maximize your earning potential as an engineer in the Philippines, there are some tips you can follow to negotiate your salary. First, make sure you have a clear understanding of your worth in the market by researching average salaries for your specialization and level of experience. It's also important to demonstrate your value to potential employers by highlighting your skills and accomplishments. Finally, be willing to negotiate and don't be afraid to ask for what you deserve.

Conclusion of How Much is Engineer Salary in the Philippines

While engineer salary in the Philippines can vary widely based on different factors, it's clear that this field has the potential to be financially rewarding. By specializing in a high-paying area of engineering, gaining experience, and negotiating your salary effectively, you can ensure that your hard work is compensated fairly. If you're passionate about engineering and interested in pursuing a career in this field, don't let concerns about salary hold you back.
