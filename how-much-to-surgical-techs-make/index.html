How Much Do Surgical Techs Make A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Eveline Sep 30, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you considering a career as a surgical technician but wondering how much you can expect to make? Look no further. In this article, we will explore the salary range of surgical techs and what factors influence their earnings.

As with any profession, salary is an important consideration. Many surgical techs may feel undervalued or unsure about what they should be earning. This can be a source of stress, especially for those just starting in their careers.

So, how much do surgical techs make? The median annual salary for surgical technologists in the United States is $48,300, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics. However, this can vary depending on several factors, such as location, experience, and education level.

In summary, surgical techs can expect to make a median salary of $48,300 per year, but this can vary depending on location, experience, and education level.

Factors That Affect Surgical Tech Salaries

Location is a significant factor when it comes to surgical tech salaries. Generally, urban areas have higher salaries than rural areas due to the higher cost of living. Additionally, some states pay more than others. For example, California and Nevada have the highest salaries for surgical techs.

Experience is also a key factor in determining surgical tech salaries. Those with more years of experience will generally earn more than those just starting.

My Personal Experience

As a surgical tech with five years of experience, I can attest to the impact of both location and experience on my salary. I work in California, where the salary range is higher than many other states. Additionally, as I gained more experience, I was able to negotiate for a higher salary.

Continuing Education

Negotiating a Higher Salary

FAQs About Surgical Tech Salaries

Q: What is the starting salary for surgical techs?

A: The starting salary for surgical techs can vary depending on location and experience, but typically ranges from $32,000 to $42,000 per year.

Q: What is the highest paying state for surgical techs?

A: California and Nevada are currently the highest paying states for surgical techs.

Q: Can continuing education courses increase my salary as a surgical tech?

A: Yes, continuing education courses can make you more valuable to your employer and increase your salary as a surgical tech.

A: Research the average salary for surgical techs in your area and use this information to negotiate for a higher salary. Consider taking continuing education courses or becoming certified to increase your value and negotiating power.

Conclusion of How Much Do Surgical Techs Make

While salary is an important consideration for any profession, it should not be the only factor when deciding whether to pursue a career as a surgical tech. However, with the right education, experience, and negotiation skills, surgical techs can earn a comfortable salary and enjoy a rewarding career in the medical field.
