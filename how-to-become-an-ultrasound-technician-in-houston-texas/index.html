How To Become An Ultrasound Technician In Houston Texas

Written by Bardi Nov 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you interested in becoming an ultrasound technician in Houston, Texas? Do you want to work in the healthcare industry and help people in a meaningful way? If so, you're in the right place. However, you might be feeling overwhelmed and unsure about where to start. That's where we come in.

Becoming an ultrasound technician can be a challenging process, but it's also incredibly rewarding. There are many factors to consider, including education, certification, and job opportunities.

To become an ultrasound technician in Houston, Texas, you'll need to complete a few essential steps. Firstly, you'll need to obtain a high school diploma or GED. Next, you'll need to complete a formal training program in diagnostic medical sonography, which typically takes two years. After completing your training, you'll need to become certified by a professional organization such as the American Registry for Diagnostic Medical Sonography (ARDMS).

In summary, becoming an ultrasound technician in Houston, Texas, requires dedication, hard work, and a commitment to your education. However, the rewards of helping people and working in the healthcare industry make it all worth it.

Education Requirements for Ultrasound Technicians in Houston Texas

One of the most critical steps in becoming an ultrasound technician in Houston, Texas, is completing the necessary educational requirements. To become an ultrasound technician, you'll need to complete a two-year diagnostic medical sonography program at an accredited institution. This program will provide you with the knowledge and skills necessary to perform ultrasound scans and interpret the results.

During your training, you'll learn about anatomy, physics, medical terminology, patient care, and more. You'll also have the opportunity to gain hands-on experience through clinical rotations.

Certification for Ultrasound Technicians in Houston Texas

After completing your educational requirements, you'll need to become certified by a professional organization such as the American Registry for Diagnostic Medical Sonography (ARDMS). Certification is essential because it demonstrates that you have the necessary knowledge and skills to perform ultrasound scans and interpret the results accurately.

To become certified, you'll need to pass an examination that tests your knowledge in various areas of diagnostic medical sonography. Once you've passed the exam, you'll be awarded the Registered Diagnostic Medical Sonographer (RDMS) credential.

Job Opportunities for Ultrasound Technicians in Houston Texas

After completing your education and certification requirements, you'll be ready to start your career as an ultrasound technician in Houston, Texas. There are many job opportunities available in hospitals, clinics, and private practices. You may also have the opportunity to specialize in a particular area of diagnostic medical sonography, such as obstetrics or cardiology.

Salary for Ultrasound Technicians in Houston Texas

The salary for ultrasound technicians in Houston, Texas, varies depending on several factors, including experience, education, and location. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual wage for diagnostic medical sonographers in Texas was $74,850 in May 2020.

Personal Experience of Becoming an Ultrasound Technician in Houston Texas

As someone who has gone through the process of becoming an ultrasound technician in Houston, Texas, I can tell you that it's not easy, but it's definitely worth it. The educational requirements were challenging, but I learned so much and gained valuable hands-on experience through clinical rotations. The certification process was also challenging, but passing the exam and becoming certified was incredibly rewarding.
