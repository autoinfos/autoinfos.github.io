How To Find Visa Sponsored Jobs In Australia A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to work in Australia but struggling to find a job that offers visa sponsorship? Look no further! In this post, we will guide you through the process of finding visa sponsored jobs in Australia in a relaxed and easy-to-understand language.

It can be frustrating to search for a job in a foreign country, especially when you need visa sponsorship. You may not know where to look or what companies offer these types of jobs. But don't worry, we've got you covered!

What is a Visa Sponsored Job?

A visa sponsored job is a position offered by an employer who is willing to sponsor a foreign worker's visa in order to work legally in Australia. This sponsorship is necessary for those who do not have Australian citizenship or permanent residency.

Now, let's dive into the steps you can take to find visa sponsored jobs in Australia.

Use Job Search Websites

One of the easiest ways to find visa sponsored jobs in Australia is by using job search websites. These websites allow you to search for jobs based on your qualifications and location. Some of the most popular job search websites in Australia include Seek, Indeed, Jora, and CareerOne.

Make sure to use keywords such as "visa sponsorship" or "sponsorship available" in your search to narrow down your results. You can also create job alerts to receive notifications when new jobs that match your criteria become available.

Network with Employers

Networking with potential employers is another effective way to find visa sponsored jobs in Australia. Attend job fairs, industry events, and join professional organizations related to your field. Networking can help you learn about job openings before they are advertised and can give you an advantage over other job seekers.

Connect with potential employers on LinkedIn and send personalized messages expressing your interest in their company and your qualifications. Don't forget to mention that you are seeking visa sponsorship!

Apply for Jobs in Regional Areas

Many employers in regional areas of Australia are struggling to find skilled workers and are more willing to sponsor visas for foreign workers. Check out job openings in regional areas and consider applying for these positions.

Regional Sponsored Migration Scheme (RSMS) is a government program that allows employers in regional areas to sponsor foreign workers for permanent residency. This can be a great option if you are looking to stay in Australia long-term.

Consider Working for Multinational Companies

Many multinational companies have offices in Australia and are more likely to offer visa sponsorship for foreign workers. Research companies that have a presence in Australia and apply for jobs that match your qualifications.

Keep in mind that the hiring process for these types of jobs may take longer, so be patient and persistent in your job search.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I apply for a visa without a job offer?

A: Yes, you can apply for a visa without a job offer. However, you will need to meet the eligibility criteria for the visa you are applying for and have sufficient funds to support yourself while in Australia.

Q: How long does it take to process a visa application?

A: The processing time for visa applications can vary depending on the type of visa and individual circumstances. It's best to check the Department of Home Affairs website for up-to-date processing times.

Q: Do all employers offer visa sponsorship?

A: No, not all employers offer visa sponsorship. However, many employers in Australia are open to sponsoring visas for skilled workers who are in demand.

Q: Can I switch jobs while on a sponsored visa?

A: If you are on a sponsored visa, you will need to check the conditions of your visa to see if you are able to switch employers. Some visas require you to work for the sponsoring employer for a certain period of time before you can switch jobs.

Conclusion of How to Find Visa Sponsored Jobs in Australia

While finding a visa sponsored job in Australia can be challenging, it's not impossible. Use job search websites, network with potential employers, consider working in regional areas, and apply for jobs with multinational companies. By following these steps, you'll increase your chances of finding a job that offers visa sponsorship.
