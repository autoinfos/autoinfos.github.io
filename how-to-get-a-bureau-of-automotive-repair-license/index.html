Types of Bureau of Automotive Repair Licenses

When it comes to getting a Bureau of Automotive Repair License, there are several types to choose from. These include:

1. Auto Body Repair: This license is required if you want to repair, replace, or paint the body of a vehicle.

2. Smog Check: This license is required if you want to perform smog checks on vehicles.

3. Brake and Lamp Inspection: This license is required if you want to inspect brakes and lamps on vehicles.

4. Automotive Repair: This license is required if you want to perform general automotive repairs on vehicles.

Each type of license has its own set of requirements, so it’s important that you choose the right one for your business.

Common Roadblocks

One of the most common roadblocks when it comes to getting a Bureau of Automotive Repair License is not meeting all the requirements. This can result in delays, or even denial of your application. Some of the requirements include:

1. Proof of insurance: You’ll need to provide proof of liability insurance for your business.

2. Business location: You’ll need to have a physical location for your business that meets certain requirements.

3. Certifications: You may need to have certain certifications or licenses, depending on the type of license you’re applying for.

4. Background check: You and your employees may need to undergo a background check.
