How To Get Autocad Certification Online A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Nov 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

If you're an aspiring interior designer, architect, or engineer, you know how important it is to have a solid foundation in Autocad. It's the industry standard for drafting and modeling software, and having a certification in Autocad can give you a competitive edge in the job market. But with the pandemic forcing many of us to stay at home, getting certified might seem like a daunting task. Fortunately, you can now get Autocad certification online, and in this post, we'll show you how.

As an aspiring designer, you might be struggling with finding the right resources to learn Autocad, or you might be hesitant to take the certification exam because of the current situation. It can be overwhelming to navigate the sea of online courses and certifications available, and it's understandable to feel uncertain about taking an exam remotely.

But don't worry, getting Autocad certification online is easier than you think. Here are the steps you need to follow:

Step 1: Choose the Right Course

Before you take the certification exam, you need to make sure you have a good grasp of the software. There are many online courses available that can help you learn Autocad, and some of them even offer certification as part of the package. Look for courses that are accredited by Autodesk, the company behind Autocad, and that have good reviews from previous students.

Step 2: Prepare for the Exam

Once you've completed the course, it's time to prepare for the exam. The certification exam is a comprehensive test that covers all aspects of Autocad, from basic commands to advanced features. Make sure you review all the material you've learned in the course and practice using the software as much as possible. Autodesk offers practice exams and study guides that can help you prepare.

Step 3: Schedule the Exam

When you're ready to take the exam, you can schedule it online through Autodesk's website. You'll need to create an account and pay the exam fee, which varies depending on the level of certification you're aiming for. The exam is proctored online, which means you'll need a stable internet connection and a webcam. Make sure you read the exam rules and requirements carefully before scheduling.

Step 4: Take the Exam

On the day of the exam, make sure you have a quiet and well-lit space to take the test. The proctor will verify your identity and check your workspace before you start. The exam is timed, and you'll need to answer a series of multiple-choice and performance-based questions. Don't worry if you don't pass the first time, you can retake the exam after a certain period.

Why Getting Autocad Certification Online is Worth It

Getting certified in Autocad can open many doors for your career. It shows potential employers that you have a solid understanding of the software and that you're committed to professional development. With so many people working remotely, having a certification in a widely used software like Autocad can give you an edge in the job market. Plus, you can learn at your own pace and take the exam from the comfort of your own home.

My Experience with Autocad Certification Online

As an interior designer, I was hesitant to take the Autocad certification exam online. But after completing an accredited course and practicing using the software, I felt confident enough to schedule the exam. I was pleasantly surprised by how smooth the process was. The proctor was professional and helpful, and the questions were fair and comprehensive. I passed the exam on my first try, and I feel much more confident in my Autocad skills now.

FAQs

Q: How much does Autocad certification cost?

A: The exam fee varies depending on the level of certification you're aiming for. The fee for the Autocad Certified User exam is $95, while the fee for the Autocad Certified Professional exam is $245.

Q: How long does the Autocad certification exam take?

A: The exam is timed, and you have 2 hours to complete it. However, the actual time it takes to finish the exam depends on your proficiency with the software.

Q: Can I retake the Autocad certification exam if I fail?

A: Yes, you can retake the exam after a certain period. The waiting period varies depending on the level of certification and the region you're in.

Q: Is Autocad certification worth it?

A: Yes, getting certified in Autocad can boost your career prospects and make you a more valuable candidate in the job market. It shows potential employers that you have a solid foundation in the software and that you're committed to professional development.

Conclusion

Getting Autocad certification online is a great way to enhance your skills as an interior designer, architect, or engineer. By following the steps we've outlined in this post, you can prepare for and take the exam from the comfort of your own home. With the pandemic forcing many of us to work remotely, having a certification in a widely used software like Autocad can give you a competitive edge in the job market. So, what are you waiting for? Start your journey to Autocad certification today!
