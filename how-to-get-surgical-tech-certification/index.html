How To Get Surgical Tech Certification

Written by Aprilia Oct 22, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you interested in pursuing a career as a surgical technologist? If so, you may be wondering how to get surgical tech certification. This process can seem overwhelming at first, but with the right guidance, it can be a straightforward and rewarding experience.

One of the main pain points when it comes to getting surgical tech certification is understanding the requirements and steps involved. It can be confusing to navigate the various organizations and programs available, as well as the specific qualifications needed to become certified.

The first step in getting surgical tech certification is to complete a recognized program in surgical technology. These programs are typically offered through vocational schools, community colleges, and universities. Once you have completed your program, you will need to pass the Certified Surgical Technologist (CST) exam offered by the National Board of Surgical Technology and Surgical Assisting (NBSTSA) in order to become certified.

Overall, the process of getting surgical tech certification involves completing a recognized program in surgical technology and passing the CST exam. Below, we will go into more detail about each of these steps and provide some helpful tips for success.

Choosing a Program in Surgical Technology

When it comes to choosing a program in surgical technology, there are a few key factors to consider. First, you will want to make sure that the program is accredited by the Commission on Accreditation of Allied Health Education Programs (CAAHEP) or the Accrediting Bureau of Health Education Schools (ABHES). This will ensure that your education meets national standards and that you will be eligible to take the CST exam upon completion.

It can also be helpful to choose a program that offers hands-on experience in a clinical setting. This will give you the opportunity to practice the skills you will need as a surgical technologist and gain valuable experience working with medical professionals.

Personally, I found that attending a community college program in surgical technology was the most effective way to gain the knowledge and experience needed to become certified. Not only did I receive a well-rounded education, but I also had the opportunity to work with experienced instructors and gain hands-on experience in a clinical setting.

Preparing for the CST Exam

Once you have completed your program in surgical technology, the next step is to prepare for the CST exam. This exam is designed to test your knowledge and skills in surgical technology, and passing it is a requirement for becoming certified.

One of the best ways to prepare for the CST exam is to use study materials provided by the NBSTSA. These materials include a candidate handbook, practice exams, and study guides. It can also be helpful to attend review courses offered by your program or other organizations.

Personally, I found that taking practice exams and reviewing the content covered in my program were the most effective ways to prepare for the CST exam. It's important to give yourself plenty of time to study and to focus on areas where you may need more practice.

Benefits of Getting Surgical Tech Certification

Getting certified as a surgical technologist can open up a range of career opportunities and benefits. For one, certification is often required by employers in order to work as a surgical technologist. It can also lead to higher pay and greater job security.

Additionally, certification can give you a sense of pride and accomplishment in your career. Knowing that you have met national standards and have the skills needed to excel in your field can be a great source of motivation and confidence.

Continuing Education and Certification Renewal

Once you have become certified as a surgical technologist, it's important to stay up-to-date with the latest developments in your field. This can be done through continuing education courses and other professional development opportunities.

In order to maintain your certification, you will also need to renew it on a regular basis. This typically involves completing a certain number of continuing education hours and meeting other requirements set by the NBSTSA.

Q&A: How to Get Surgical Tech Certification

Q: What are the requirements for getting surgical tech certification?

A: The main requirements are completing a recognized program in surgical technology and passing the CST exam offered by the NBSTSA.

Q: How long does it take to become certified as a surgical technologist?

A: The length of time can vary depending on the program you choose and the pace at which you complete it. However, most programs take between 9 months and 2 years to complete.

Q: What are some tips for success in getting certified?

A: Some tips include choosing a reputable program, studying diligently for the CST exam, and staying up-to-date with the latest developments in your field.

Q: Is certification required to work as a surgical technologist?

A: Certification is not always required, but it is often preferred by employers and can lead to greater job opportunities and benefits.

Conclusion of How to Get Surgical Tech Certification

Getting certified as a surgical technologist can be a challenging but rewarding process. By choosing a reputable program, studying diligently for the CST exam, and staying up-to-date with the latest developments in your field, you can achieve your goal of becoming a certified surgical technologist and enjoying a fulfilling career in healthcare.
