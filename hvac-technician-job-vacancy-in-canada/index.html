What Are Some Common HVAC Technician Duties?

As an HVAC technician, you will be responsible for a variety of duties. Some common tasks include installing new HVAC systems, repairing existing ones, and performing regular maintenance. You may also need to troubleshoot issues and order replacement parts. Additionally, you will need to communicate with customers and other team members to ensure projects are completed on time and within budget.
