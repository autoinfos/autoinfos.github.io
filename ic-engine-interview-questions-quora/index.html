What are some frequently asked IC engine interview questions?

1. What is an IC engine, and how does it work?

Answer: An IC engine is an internal combustion engine that works by burning fuel inside a combustion chamber. The heat generated by the combustion process is used to create pressure, which is then used to move a piston or turbine.

2. What are the different types of IC engines?

Answer: The different types of IC engines include spark-ignition engines, compression-ignition engines, and gas turbines.

3. What is the difference between a two-stroke and four-stroke engine?

Answer: A two-stroke engine completes a cycle in two strokes of the piston, while a four-stroke engine completes a cycle in four strokes of the piston.

4. What are the different components of an IC engine?

Answer: The different components of an IC engine include the combustion chamber, cylinder, piston, connecting rod, crankshaft, valves, and camshaft.
