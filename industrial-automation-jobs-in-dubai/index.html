Industrial Automation Jobs In Dubai Opportunities And Challenges

Written by Daniel Jul 20, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a career in industrial automation? Dubai might just be the perfect place for you. With its growing economy and booming industries, Dubai offers a wide range of job opportunities for automation professionals. In this blog post, we will discuss the challenges and opportunities of industrial automation jobs in Dubai, and provide insights on how to succeed in this field.

The industrial automation field in Dubai is not without its challenges. One of the biggest pain points is the shortage of skilled professionals. Many companies are struggling to find qualified candidates to fill their automation positions. Additionally, the rapid pace of technological advancements in this field makes it difficult for professionals to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies.

Despite these challenges, there is a high demand for automation professionals in Dubai. The city's growing economy has led to a surge in automation projects across various industries, including manufacturing, construction, and oil and gas. The target of industrial automation jobs in Dubai is to develop and deploy automation systems that enhance productivity, efficiency, and safety in these industries.

In summary, industrial automation jobs in Dubai offer both opportunities and challenges. The shortage of skilled professionals and the fast pace of technological advancements can make it difficult to succeed in this field. However, the high demand for automation professionals and the growing economy of Dubai make it a promising career choice.

Target of Industrial Automation Jobs in Dubai

The target of industrial automation jobs in Dubai is to design, develop, and deploy automation systems that improve the efficiency, productivity, and safety of various industries. From manufacturing plants to oil refineries, automation professionals play a crucial role in optimizing operations and reducing costs.

I started my career in industrial automation as a controls engineer in a manufacturing plant. My job was to design and implement automation systems that controlled the production process. This involved programming PLCs, designing HMI interfaces, and integrating various sensors and control devices. It was a challenging job, but also very rewarding. I knew that my work was making a real difference in the efficiency and productivity of the plant.

Skills Required for Industrial Automation Jobs in Dubai

To succeed in industrial automation jobs in Dubai, you need to have a strong foundation in electrical engineering, computer science, or a related field. You should also have a good understanding of automation technologies, such as PLCs, HMIs, and SCADA systems. Additionally, you should be able to work effectively in a team environment and communicate technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.

Top Industrial Automation Companies in Dubai

If you're looking for industrial automation jobs in Dubai, here are some of the top companies to consider:

  • Siemens
  • ABB
  • Honeywell
  • Emerson
  • Schneider Electric

How to Succeed in Industrial Automation Jobs in Dubai

To succeed in industrial automation jobs in Dubai, you need to be willing to continuously learn and adapt to new technologies. This field is constantly evolving, and you need to keep up with the latest trends and advancements. Additionally, you should network with other professionals in the industry and attend conferences and workshops to expand your knowledge and skills.

Salary and Benefits of Industrial Automation Jobs in Dubai

The salary and benefits of industrial automation jobs in Dubai vary depending on the level of experience and the specific job role. On average, automation engineers in Dubai earn between AED 120,000 and AED 240,000 per year. Companies also offer benefits such as health insurance, vacation time, and retirement plans.
