Common Instrument Technician Interview Questions and Answers in Hindi PDF

Here are four common instrument technician interview questions and answers in Hindi PDF:

Q: What is a PID controller?

A: A PID controller is a type of feedback controller commonly used in industrial control systems. It stands for "proportional-integral-derivative" and is used to maintain a desired setpoint by adjusting a control variable in response to feedback from a process variable. The proportional term responds to the immediate error, the integral term responds to the accumulation of past errors, and the derivative term responds to the rate of change of the error.

Q: What is the purpose of a thermocouple?

A: A thermocouple is a type of temperature sensor used in industrial processes to measure temperature. It works by generating a voltage proportional to the difference in temperature between two junctions of dissimilar metals. The voltage can then be used to calculate the temperature of the system being measured.

Q: How would you troubleshoot a malfunctioning pressure transmitter?

A: If a pressure transmitter is malfunctioning, the first step is to check the power supply and wiring connections to ensure they are properly connected and functioning. Next, check the calibration settings to make sure they are correct. If the transmitter is still not functioning properly, it may be necessary to replace the sensor or transmitter module.

Q: What is your experience with PLC programming?

A: I have extensive experience with PLC programming, including programming ladder logic diagrams, configuring I/O modules, and troubleshooting communication issues. I am also familiar with a variety of PLC programming languages, including ladder logic, structured text, and function block diagrams.
