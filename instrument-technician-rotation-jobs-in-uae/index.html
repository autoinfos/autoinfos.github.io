What Does an Instrument Technician Do?

Instrument technicians are responsible for installing, maintaining, and repairing the equipment that is used to control and measure various processes in a variety of industries. This can include everything from temperature sensors to flow meters to control panels. Some of the key skills required for this job include an understanding of electrical systems, the ability to read and interpret technical drawings and schematics, and knowledge of various types of instrumentation and control equipment.
