Q. What is the difference between two-stroke and four-stroke engines?

A. Two-stroke engines complete a power cycle in two strokes, while four-stroke engines complete it in four strokes.

Q. What is the function of a turbocharger?

A. A turbocharger increases the engine's power output by forcing more air into the combustion chamber.

Q. What would you do if the engine breaks down in the middle of the ocean?

A. I would try to identify the problem and fix it if possible. If not, I would send a distress signal and take necessary measures to ensure the safety of the crew.

Q. What are your strengths and weaknesses?
