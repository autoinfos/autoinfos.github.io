What is APHR Certification?

APHR stands for Associate Professional in Human Resources. It is a certification offered by the HR Certification Institute (HRCI) to individuals who demonstrate their knowledge and expertise in the field of human resources. This certification is recognized globally and can help you advance your career in HR.

As an interior designer, you might be wondering why we are discussing APHR certification. Well, the HRCI also offers a certification program specifically for professionals in the interior design industry. This program is called the Associate Professional in Interior Design (APID) certification, which is often referred to as APHR certification in the industry.

APID certification can help you enhance your skills and knowledge in areas such as space planning, color psychology, and building codes. It can also help you gain recognition and credibility in the industry, which can lead to more job opportunities and higher pay.

Q: How much does it cost to obtain APHR certification?

A: The cost of APHR certification varies depending on your location and the testing center you choose. You can expect to pay around $400 for the exam and additional fees for study materials and application.

Q: How long does it take to get APHR certification?
