What Are the Visa Requirements for IT Jobs in Australia for Indians?

The visa requirements for IT jobs in Australia for Indians depend on the type of visa you want to apply for. The most common visa for skilled workers is the skilled independent visa (subclass 189). To be eligible for this visa, you must have a skill that is in demand in Australia and meet other requirements, such as age and language proficiency.

Another option is the employer-sponsored visa (subclass 482). This visa allows Australian employers to sponsor skilled workers from overseas to work in Australia. To be eligible for this visa, you must have a job offer from an Australian employer and meet other requirements.
