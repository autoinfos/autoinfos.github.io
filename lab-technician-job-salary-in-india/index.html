Lab technicians are an essential part of the healthcare system. However, despite the critical role they play, lab technicians often face challenges related to their salaries.

The average lab technician job salary in India is around INR 2.5-3 lakhs per annum. However, this can vary depending on factors such as experience, location, and qualifications. Many lab technicians in India face low salaries, long working hours, and limited opportunities for career growth.

In summary, lab technician job salary in India is an important consideration for anyone considering a career in this field. How to Improve Your Lab Technician Job Salary in India
