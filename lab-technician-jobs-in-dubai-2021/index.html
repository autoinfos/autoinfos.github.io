Question and Answer

What qualifications do I need to become a lab technician in Dubai?

To become a lab technician in Dubai, you will typically need a degree or diploma in laboratory sciences, such as chemistry, biology, or medical laboratory technology. Relevant work experience is also important, as well as strong communication and teamwork skills.

What is the salary range for lab technician jobs in Dubai 2021?

The salary range for lab technician jobs in Dubai 2021 varies depending on factors such as your level of experience, qualifications, and the specific industry or organization you work for. However, salaries are generally competitive and can range from AED 5,000 to AED 20,000 per month.

What are some of the challenges of working as a lab technician in Dubai?

Working as a lab technician in Dubai can be challenging at times due to long hours, demanding workloads, and the need to stay up-to-date on the latest scientific advances and technologies. Some of the benefits of working as a lab technician in Dubai include the high demand for skilled professionals in this field, competitive salaries and benefits packages, and the opportunity to work on cutting-edge research and development projects.
