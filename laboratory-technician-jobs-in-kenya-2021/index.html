Laboratory Technician Jobs In Kenya 2021 Career Opportunities And Requirements

Written by Daniel Oct 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking for a career in the medical field? Do you have a passion for science and technology? If so, then laboratory technician jobs in Kenya 2021 may be the perfect fit for you. As a laboratory technician, you will work in a laboratory setting and perform various tests and experiments to help diagnose and treat diseases.

However, there are some challenges that come with this profession. Laboratory technicians often work long hours and are required to stand for extended periods of time. They also handle hazardous materials that require strict safety protocols. Additionally, the job can be mentally demanding as it requires attention to detail and the ability to work under pressure.

To become a laboratory technician in Kenya, you must have a diploma or degree in medical laboratory science from a recognized institution. You also need to be registered with the Kenya Medical Laboratory Technicians and Technologists Board. Additionally, you will need to have good communication skills, be able to work well in a team, and have a strong work ethic.

In summary, laboratory technician jobs in Kenya 2021 offer a rewarding career for those who are passionate about science and technology. However, it requires hard work and dedication to succeed in this field.

What are the Career Opportunities for Laboratory Technicians in Kenya?

There are many career opportunities for laboratory technicians in Kenya. You can work in public or private hospitals, research institutions, and even in the pharmaceutical industry. Additionally, there is a growing demand for laboratory technicians in the country, which means that there are many job opportunities available.

What is the Salary Range for Laboratory Technicians in Kenya?

According to payscale.com, the average salary for a laboratory technician in Kenya is Ksh 350,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on your level of experience, the institution you work for, and other factors.
