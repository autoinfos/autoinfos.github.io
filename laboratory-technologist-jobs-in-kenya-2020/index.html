What is a Laboratory Technologist?

A laboratory technologist is a medical professional who performs laboratory tests and experiments to aid in the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of diseases. They are responsible for handling and analyzing samples of blood, tissue, and other bodily fluids, as well as maintaining and calibrating laboratory equipment. A laboratory technologist must have a strong understanding of biology, chemistry, and other scientific principles, as well as excellent attention to detail and organizational skills.
