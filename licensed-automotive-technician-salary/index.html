What Is The Average Salary Of A Licensed Automotive Technician

Written by Robby Jul 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

When it comes to working as a licensed automotive technician, salary is always a concern. Many people wonder how much they can expect to earn in this profession, and whether or not it's a financially viable career choice. Unfortunately, the answer to this question isn't always straightforward.

While automotive technicians are in high demand, and the job market is expected to grow steadily over the next decade, salaries can vary depending on a number of factors. This can include the technician's level of experience, the location of the job, and the type of vehicles they specialize in.

According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the average salary for automotive service technicians and mechanics was $44,050 per year as of May 2020. However, this number can range from $25,000 to $69,000 per year depending on the factors listed above.

Personal Experience in Licensed Automotive Technician Salary

As someone who has worked as a licensed automotive technician for over a decade, I can tell you that salaries can vary widely in this field. When I first started out, I was making around $30,000 per year. However, as I gained more experience and developed a specialty in high-end European vehicles, my salary increased to over $60,000 per year.

Factors Affecting Licensed Automotive Technician Salary

As previously mentioned, there are a number of factors that can affect the salary of a licensed automotive technician. One of the biggest factors is experience. Technicians with more years of experience and a proven track record of success are often able to command higher salaries than those who are just starting out.

Another factor is location. Salaries can vary widely depending on where you live and work. In general, technicians in urban areas tend to make more than those in rural areas. This is largely due to the fact that urban areas have a higher cost of living, and employers need to pay their technicians more to attract and retain top talent.

Specialization in Licensed Automotive Technician Salary

The type of vehicles that a licensed automotive technician specializes in can also affect their salary. Technicians who specialize in high-end luxury vehicles or exotic sports cars, for example, often make more than those who work on more common makes and models.

How to Increase Your Licensed Automotive Technician Salary

If you're looking to increase your salary as a licensed automotive technician, there are a few things you can do. First and foremost, you can work on developing your skills and expertise. The more specialized you are, the more valuable you will be to employers.

You can also consider moving to a new location. If you're currently working in a rural area where salaries are lower, consider moving to an urban area where salaries are higher. Additionally, you can negotiate your salary with your current employer or look for new job opportunities that offer higher pay.

Question and Answer

Q: Can licensed automotive technicians make six-figure salaries?

A: While it's possible for licensed automotive technicians to make six-figure salaries, it's relatively uncommon. This typically requires many years of experience, a high level of expertise, and a specialization in working on high-end vehicles.

Q: How does the salary of a licensed automotive technician compare to other trades?

A: The salary of a licensed automotive technician is generally on par with other skilled trades, such as electricians and plumbers. However, it's important to note that salaries can vary widely depending on a number of factors.

Q: Do licensed automotive technicians receive benefits?

A: Many licensed automotive technicians receive benefits such as health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off. However, this can vary depending on the employer.

Q: Is it possible to work part-time as a licensed automotive technician?

A: While many automotive technicians work full-time, it is possible to work part-time in this field. This can be a good option for those who are looking for a more flexible work schedule.

Conclusion of Licensed Automotive Technician Salary

Overall, the salary of a licensed automotive technician can vary widely depending on a number of factors. However, with the right skills and expertise, it's possible to earn a comfortable living in this field. Whether you're just starting out or looking to increase your salary, there are a number of strategies you can use to achieve your goals.
