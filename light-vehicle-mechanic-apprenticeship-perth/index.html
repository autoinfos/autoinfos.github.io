How to Start Your Light Vehicle Mechanic Apprenticeship in Perth?

To start your light vehicle mechanic apprenticeship in Perth, you'll need to find an employer who is willing to take you on as an apprentice. You can do this by searching online job boards, contacting local mechanics and garages, or contacting the Western Australian Apprenticeship Centre. Once you've found an employer, you'll need to sign a training contract and register with an apprenticeship network provider.

How to Become a Qualified Light Vehicle Mechanic in Perth?

To become a qualified light vehicle mechanic in Perth, you'll need to complete your apprenticeship and obtain a Certificate III in Light Vehicle Mechanical Technology. This certificate is recognised nationally and is a requirement for becoming a qualified mechanic. After obtaining your certificate, you can apply for a motor vehicle repairer's licence, which allows you to work as a mechanic in Western Australia.
