Mechanical Design Engineer Salary In Saudi Arabia All You Need To Know

Written by Aprilia Sep 21, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you a mechanical design engineer looking for a job in Saudi Arabia? Or are you already working there and wondering if you're being paid enough? Either way, you're in the right place. In this post, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about mechanical design engineer salary in Saudi Arabia.

Working as a mechanical design engineer in Saudi Arabia can be rewarding, but it also comes with its own set of challenges. One of these challenges is understanding the salary structure and how it compares to other countries. You might find that the salary package offered to you is not what you expected, or it might be difficult to negotiate for a better salary.

So, what is the average salary for a mechanical design engineer in Saudi Arabia? According to data from Glassdoor, the average salary for a mechanical design engineer in Saudi Arabia is SAR 128,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on your education, experience, and the company you work for.

In summary, mechanical design engineer salary in Saudi Arabia can be competitive, but it's important to do your research and negotiate effectively to get the best package possible. Keep in mind that salary is just one aspect of a job, and other factors like work-life balance, benefits, and company culture should also be taken into account.

Factors That Affect Mechanical Design Engineer Salary in Saudi Arabia

Now that we know the average salary for a mechanical design engineer in Saudi Arabia, let's take a closer look at the factors that can affect this salary:

Education: The higher your education level, the more likely you are to earn a higher salary. For example, if you have a master's or PhD in mechanical engineering, you could potentially earn more than someone with just a bachelor's degree.

Experience: The more experience you have, the more valuable you are to the company. If you have several years of experience working as a mechanical design engineer, you could negotiate for a higher salary than someone who is just starting out.

Industry: The industry you work in can also affect your salary. For example, working in the oil and gas industry could potentially offer a higher salary than working in the automotive industry.

Company: The company you work for can also play a role in determining your salary. Larger, multinational companies tend to offer higher salaries and better benefits than smaller, local companies.

Personal Experience

As a mechanical design engineer who has worked in Saudi Arabia for several years, I can say that the salary packages offered by companies can vary widely. Some companies offer very competitive packages that include housing allowances, transportation allowances, and other benefits. Other companies offer lower salaries with fewer benefits.

One thing to keep in mind is that the cost of living in Saudi Arabia can also vary depending on the city you're in. For example, living in Riyadh can be more expensive than living in Jeddah. It's important to factor in the cost of living when negotiating your salary package.

Tips for Negotiating Mechanical Design Engineer Salary in Saudi Arabia

When negotiating your salary as a mechanical design engineer in Saudi Arabia, here are some tips to keep in mind:

Do your research: Look up the average salary for your position and industry to get a better idea of what you should be earning. Websites like Glassdoor and Salary Explorer can be helpful for this.

Highlight your experience and education: Make sure to emphasize your qualifications and experience when negotiating your salary. The more valuable you are to the company, the more likely they are to offer a higher salary.

Consider other benefits: Don't just focus on salary when negotiating your package. Other benefits like housing allowances, transportation allowances, and health insurance can also be important.

Question and Answer

Q: Is the cost of living in Saudi Arabia high?

A: The cost of living in Saudi Arabia can vary depending on the city you're in. Generally, larger cities like Riyadh and Jeddah are more expensive than smaller cities.

Q: What is the average salary for a mechanical design engineer in Saudi Arabia?

A: According to Glassdoor, the average salary for a mechanical design engineer in Saudi Arabia is SAR 128,000 per year.

Q: How can I negotiate for a higher salary?

A: Do your research, highlight your experience and education, and consider other benefits like housing allowances and health insurance.

Q: What industries offer the highest salaries for mechanical design engineers in Saudi Arabia?

A: The oil and gas industry tends to offer higher salaries than other industries, but this can vary depending on the company and location.

Conclusion of Mechanical Design Engineer Salary in Saudi Arabia

In conclusion, mechanical design engineer salary in Saudi Arabia can be competitive, but it's important to do your research and negotiate effectively to get the best package possible. Factors like education, experience, industry, and company can all affect your salary. Keep in mind that salary is just one aspect of a job, and other factors like work-life balance, benefits, and company culture should also be taken into account.
