So, what can you expect to earn as a mechanical engineer in Dubai? Entry-level mechanical engineers can expect to earn around AED 60,000 per year, while senior-level engineers can earn upwards of AED 300,000 per year.

In conclusion, mechanical engineer jobs in Dubai salary can be quite lucrative, but it's important to do your research and consider all factors before making a decision. Keep in mind that the cost of living in Dubai is high, and taxes can eat into your earnings.
