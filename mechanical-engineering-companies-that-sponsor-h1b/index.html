Question and Answer

Q: Can any mechanical engineering company sponsor H1B visas?

Q: What are some of the requirements for obtaining an H1B visa?

A: To obtain an H1B visa, you must have a job offer from a US employer, possess specialized skills or knowledge, and have a bachelor's degree or higher in the field of your employment.

Q: How long does it take to obtain an H1B visa?
