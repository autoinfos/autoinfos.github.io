Types of Mechanical Technician Programs

There are several types of mechanical technician programs available, including certificate programs, diploma programs, and degree programs. Certificate programs are typically the shortest and most focused programs, often taking less than a year to complete. Diploma programs take slightly longer and provide a more comprehensive education. Degree programs, such as an Associate's or Bachelor's degree, take the longest to complete but offer the most comprehensive education.
