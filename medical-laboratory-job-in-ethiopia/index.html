Types of Medical Laboratory Job in Ethiopia

There are a variety of medical laboratory job in Ethiopia available, ranging from entry-level positions to more advanced roles. Some common types of medical laboratory job in Ethiopia include:

Medical Laboratory Technician

Medical laboratory technicians perform routine laboratory tests and procedures under the supervision of a medical technologist or pathologist. They may work in a variety of settings, including hospitals, clinics, and research facilities.

How to Become a Medical Laboratory Technician

To become a medical laboratory technician, you typically need an associate's degree in medical laboratory technology or a related field. You may also be required to obtain certification or licensure in your state.

Medical Laboratory Technologist

Medical laboratory technologists perform more complex tests and procedures than medical laboratory technicians. They may also supervise medical laboratory technicians and other staff members. Medical laboratory technologists typically work in hospitals or large medical facilities.

How to Become a Medical Laboratory Technologist

To become a medical laboratory technologist, you typically need a bachelor's degree in medical laboratory science or a related field. You may also be required to obtain certification or licensure in your state.

Q: What skills are necessary for medical laboratory job in Ethiopia?
