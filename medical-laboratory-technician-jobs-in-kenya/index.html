Discovering Medical Laboratory Technician Jobs In Kenya

Written by Robby Jun 16, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you interested in pursuing a career in the medical field? Do you have a passion for laboratory work and attention to detail? If so, then medical laboratory technician jobs in Kenya may be the perfect fit for you.

Working as a medical laboratory technician in Kenya can present a unique set of challenges and rewards. Some of the pain points that come with the job include working long hours, dealing with patients who may be ill or in pain, and working with potentially hazardous materials. However, the rewards of the job can be immeasurable, such as the satisfaction of helping to diagnose and treat illnesses, working in a constantly evolving field, and having the potential to make a positive impact on people's lives.

So, what exactly is a medical laboratory technician? This role involves performing tests and analyses on bodily fluids, tissues, and other substances to help diagnose and treat illnesses. They work closely with other medical professionals, such as doctors and nurses, to ensure accurate and timely results. In Kenya, medical laboratory technicians are required to be registered with the Kenya Medical Laboratory Technicians and Technologists Board (KMLTTB) in order to practice legally.

To summarize, medical laboratory technician jobs in Kenya offer a challenging yet rewarding career path for those interested in the medical field. While there may be some pain points associated with the job, the potential rewards make it a worthwhile pursuit. Let's dive deeper into what this career entails.

What is a Medical Laboratory Technician?

As previously mentioned, a medical laboratory technician performs tests and analyses on bodily fluids, tissues, and other substances to help diagnose and treat illnesses. They use sophisticated equipment and technology to analyze samples and communicate their findings to other medical professionals. In Kenya, medical laboratory technicians are responsible for ensuring that all test results are accurate, timely, and comply with the standards set by the KMLTTB.

Personally, I have always been fascinated by the inner workings of the human body and the diagnostic tools used to detect and treat illnesses. Pursuing a career as a medical laboratory technician has allowed me to turn that fascination into a fulfilling and rewarding career.

What are the Requirements for Medical Laboratory Technician Jobs in Kenya?

In order to practice as a medical laboratory technician in Kenya, one must be registered with the KMLTTB. This involves completing a recognized medical laboratory technician program and passing the KMLTTB exams. Additionally, medical laboratory technicians must adhere to the standards set by the KMLTTB and attend continuing education courses to stay up to date with the latest developments and technologies in the field.

What Skills are Required for Medical Laboratory Technician Jobs in Kenya?

Medical laboratory technicians in Kenya must possess a strong attention to detail, as accuracy is paramount in this field. They must also have excellent communication and interpersonal skills, as they will be working closely with other medical professionals and patients. Additionally, medical laboratory technicians should have a good understanding of laboratory safety procedures and the ability to work well under pressure.

How to Find Medical Laboratory Technician Jobs in Kenya?

There are a variety of ways to find medical laboratory technician jobs in Kenya. One option is to search online job boards or career websites, such as BrighterMonday, MyJobMag, or Fuzu. Another option is to network with other medical professionals or reach out to local hospitals or clinics to inquire about job openings. It may also be helpful to attend job fairs or industry events to meet potential employers and learn more about the field.

What is the Salary Range for Medical Laboratory Technician Jobs in Kenya?

The salary range for medical laboratory technician jobs in Kenya can vary depending on a variety of factors, such as experience, location, and employer. On average, medical laboratory technicians in Kenya can expect to earn between Ksh 20,000 to Ksh 80,000 per month.

Question and Answer Section

Q: What are some common tests that medical laboratory technicians perform?

A: Some common tests include blood tests, urine tests, tissue analysis, and microbiology tests.

Q: What is the job outlook for medical laboratory technician jobs in Kenya?

A: According to the Kenya National Bureau of Statistics, the healthcare and social assistance sector is expected to experience significant job growth in the coming years, which could lead to increased demand for medical laboratory technicians.

Q: What are some potential career paths for medical laboratory technicians in Kenya?

A: With additional education and training, medical laboratory technicians can pursue careers as medical laboratory technologists or biomedical scientists. They may also choose to specialize in a particular area of laboratory work, such as microbiology or hematology.

Q: What are some challenges facing medical laboratory technicians in Kenya?

A: Some challenges include limited resources and funding for laboratory equipment and supplies, a shortage of qualified personnel, and a lack of public awareness about the importance of laboratory work in healthcare.

Conclusion of Medical Laboratory Technician Jobs in Kenya

Medical laboratory technician jobs in Kenya offer a challenging and rewarding career path for those interested in the medical field. While there may be some pain points associated with the job, the potential rewards make it a worthwhile pursuit. By obtaining the necessary education and training, as well as staying up to date with the latest developments in the field, medical laboratory technicians can make a positive impact on people's lives and contribute to the healthcare industry in Kenya.
