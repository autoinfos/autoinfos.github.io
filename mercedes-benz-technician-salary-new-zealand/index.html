What is the Average Mercedes Benz Technician Salary in New Zealand?

When it comes to determining the average Mercedes Benz technician salary in New Zealand, there are a few things to keep in mind. According to Payscale, the average hourly wage for a Mercedes Benz technician in New Zealand is NZD 24.22. This translates to an annual salary of around NZD 50,000 to NZD 70,000, depending on your level of experience.

However, it's important to note that this is just an average, and your salary can vary widely depending on factors like your location, employer, and level of experience.
