The Target of Mixer Truck Driver Jobs in Abu Dhabi

Qualifications for Mixer Truck Driver Jobs in Abu Dhabi

To be eligible for a mixer truck driver job in Abu Dhabi, you must have a valid UAE driving license and be able to read and write in English. You should also have experience driving heavy vehicles and be familiar with the rules and regulations of the road. Additionally, you must have a clean driving record and be able to pass a drug test.

Job Responsibilities of Mixer Truck Driver Jobs in Abu Dhabi

The job responsibilities of a mixer truck driver in Abu Dhabi include:

  • Transporting ready-mix concrete from the batch plant to the construction site
  • Ensuring that the concrete is delivered on time and meets the required standards
  • Maintaining the cleanliness and safety of the truck
  • Completing daily logs and reports

How to Apply for Mixer Truck Driver Jobs in Abu Dhabi

If you meet the qualifications and are interested in applying for a mixer truck driver job in Abu Dhabi, you can search for job openings on various job portals or contact construction companies directly. Salary Expectations for Mixer Truck Driver Jobs in Abu Dhabi

The salary for mixer truck driver jobs in Abu Dhabi varies depending on the company and your level of experience. However, the average salary ranges from AED 4,000 to AED 6,000 per month, with some companies offering additional benefits such as housing and transportation allowances.
