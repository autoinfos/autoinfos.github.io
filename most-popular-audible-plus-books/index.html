The Newcomer: “The Alchemist” by Paulo Coelho

“The Alchemist” is a classic novel that has been translated into 80 languages. The audiobook version is narrated by Jeremy Irons and has received rave reviews from Audible users. The story follows a young shepherd named Santiago as he embarks on a journey to find his purpose in life. Along the way, he meets different characters who teach him valuable life lessons. “The Alchemist” is an inspiring and thought-provoking book that will leave you feeling motivated to chase your dreams.

The Bestseller: “The Girl on the Train” by Paula Hawkins

“The Girl on the Train” has been a bestseller since its release in 2015. The audiobook version is narrated by Clare Corbett, Louise Brealey, and India Fisher. The story follows Rachel, a woman who takes the same train every day and becomes obsessed with a couple she sees from the train window. When the woman goes missing, Rachel becomes a suspect in the investigation. “The Girl on the Train” is a gripping thriller that will keep you on the edge of your seat.
