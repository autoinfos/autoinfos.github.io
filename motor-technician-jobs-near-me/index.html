The Different Types of Motor Technician Jobs

There are several different types of motor technician jobs available, depending on your interests and level of experience. Some common positions include:

  • General Service Technician: This position involves performing routine maintenance tasks such as oil changes, tire rotations, and brake inspections.
  • Diagnostic Technician: Diagnostic technicians use specialized tools and equipment to identify and repair complex issues with a vehicle's engine, transmission, or other systems.
  • Specialty Technician: Specialty technicians focus on specific types of vehicles, such as diesel trucks or high-performance sports cars.
  • Service Advisor: Service advisors work directly with customers to schedule appointments, provide estimates, and answer questions about their vehicles.
