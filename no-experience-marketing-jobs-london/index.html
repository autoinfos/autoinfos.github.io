No Experience Marketing Jobs In London Your Gateway To The Marketing Industry

Written by Petter Aug 22, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a recent graduate looking to enter the exciting world of marketing? Or perhaps you're looking for a career change and want to explore the possibilities of a marketing job? Regardless of your situation, finding a marketing job in London without any experience can be daunting. But don't worry, there are opportunities out there for you!

One of the biggest pain points for job seekers is the lack of experience required for entry-level marketing jobs. Many companies are looking for candidates with a few years of experience under their belt, leaving recent graduates and career changers feeling stuck.

However, there are ways to break into the marketing industry without any prior experience. It's all about positioning yourself in the right way and taking advantage of the resources available to you.

In this article, we'll explore the world of no experience marketing jobs in London and give you tips on how to land your dream job in this exciting field.

No Experience Marketing Jobs London: What's the Target?

The target of no experience marketing jobs in London is to provide opportunities for recent graduates and career changers to break into the marketing industry. These jobs typically require little to no prior experience and are designed to give candidates the chance to learn on the job.

My personal experience with no experience marketing jobs in London was a positive one. After graduating with a degree in English Literature, I knew I wanted to pursue a career in marketing but had no idea where to start. I applied for an entry-level marketing assistant position at a small agency and was hired despite having no prior marketing experience. The job allowed me to learn the ropes of the industry and paved the way for future career opportunities.

No Experience Marketing Jobs London: Where to Look

When it comes to finding no experience marketing jobs in London, there are a few key places to look. Firstly, check out job boards such as Indeed, Reed, and Totaljobs. These sites often have a dedicated section for entry-level marketing jobs and allow you to filter by location to find roles in London.

You can also check out the websites of marketing agencies and companies you're interested in working for. Many of them will have a careers section on their website where you can apply for entry-level positions.

No Experience Marketing Jobs London: What to Expect

So, what can you expect from a no experience marketing job in London? These jobs typically involve a mix of administrative tasks and hands-on marketing work. You'll likely be supporting more senior members of the marketing team and learning on the job.

Tasks may include things like creating social media posts, conducting market research, and assisting with the planning and execution of marketing campaigns. While the work may not be glamorous, it's a great way to get your foot in the door and start building up your experience.

Tips for Landing a No Experience Marketing Job in London

So, how can you increase your chances of landing a no experience marketing job in London? Here are a few tips:

  1. Build up your transferable skills. Even if you don't have any specific marketing experience, you may have skills that are transferable to the marketing industry. For example, if you have experience with social media or content creation, make sure to highlight this on your CV and in your cover letter.
  2. Network, network, network. Attend industry events and connect with professionals on LinkedIn. You never know who may be able to help you land your dream job.
  3. Be willing to start at the bottom. Entry-level jobs may not be the most glamorous, but they can be a stepping stone to bigger and better things. Be willing to put in the work and learn as much as you can.

No Experience Marketing Jobs London: Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need a marketing degree to land a no experience marketing job in London?

A: While having a marketing degree can be helpful, it's not always necessary. Many no experience marketing jobs in London are open to candidates from a variety of backgrounds.

Q: What kind of salary can I expect from a no experience marketing job in London?

A: Salaries for entry-level marketing jobs in London can vary, but you can expect to earn around £20,000 - £25,000 per year.

Q: Will I be able to progress in my career from a no experience marketing job?

A: Absolutely! Entry-level jobs are often a stepping stone to more senior positions in the industry. If you're willing to put in the work and learn as much as you can, there's no reason why you can't progress in your career.

Q: Are there any downsides to no experience marketing jobs in London?

A: Like any job, there may be downsides to entry-level marketing jobs. These may include things like low pay, long hours, and repetitive tasks. However, these jobs are a great way to get your foot in the door and start building up your experience.

Conclusion of No Experience Marketing Jobs London

If you're looking to break into the marketing industry but don't have any prior experience, don't despair. There are plenty of no experience marketing jobs in London available for recent graduates and career changers. By positioning yourself in the right way and taking advantage of the resources available to you, you can land your dream job in this exciting field.
