What are Own Car Jobs in Dubai?

Own car jobs in Dubai refer to the jobs that allow you to use your car to provide transportation services to people. These jobs are mainly available in the ride-hailing industry, where drivers use their vehicles to provide rides to passengers.

How to Become an Own Car Driver in Dubai?

If you want to become an own car driver in Dubai, you need to follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Obtain a UAE Driving License

The first step to becoming an own car driver in Dubai is to obtain a UAE driving license. You can either apply for a new license or convert your existing license if you are from a country that has a reciprocal agreement with the UAE.

Step 2: Choose a Ride-Hailing Company

Once you have your driving license, you need to choose a ride-hailing company to work with. There are several ride-hailing companies in Dubai, including Uber, Careem, and Bolt. You can choose the one that suits your needs the best.

Step 3: Register with the Ride-Hailing Company

After selecting a ride-hailing company, you need to register with them. The registration process is straightforward and can be done online through the company's website or mobile app.

Step 4: Get Your Car Inspected

Before you can start driving for the ride-hailing company, you need to get your car inspected. The inspection is to ensure that your car meets the safety and quality standards set by the company.
