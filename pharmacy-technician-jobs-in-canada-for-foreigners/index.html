What are Pharmacy Technician Jobs in Canada for Foreigners?

Pharmacy technician jobs in Canada for foreigners are job opportunities available for foreign-trained pharmacy technicians to work in Canada. These jobs require pharmacy technicians to assist pharmacists in dispensing medications, managing inventory, and providing customer service to patients.

As a foreign-trained pharmacy technician, I faced many challenges when I first started working in Canada. However, with time and practice, I was able to improve my language skills and adapt to the Canadian work environment.

What are the Requirements for Pharmacy Technician Jobs in Canada for Foreigners?

To work as a pharmacy technician in Canada, you must meet the following requirements:

Education and Training

You must have completed a pharmacy technician program at a recognized institution and hold a diploma or certificate. Your program must include theoretical and practical courses in pharmaceutical calculations, drug distribution systems, pharmacology, and pharmacy law.

Certification

You must be certified by the Pharmacy Examining Board of Canada (PEBC). The PEBC evaluates the qualifications of foreign-trained pharmacy technicians and provides certification to those who meet their standards. To be eligible for certification, you must have completed a pharmacy technician program at a recognized institution and have relevant work experience.

Language Proficiency

You must be proficient in English or French. The level of proficiency required depends on the province or territory where you plan to work. You may need to take a language proficiency test to demonstrate your skills.

How to Find Pharmacy Technician Jobs in Canada for Foreigners?

There are several ways to find pharmacy technician jobs in Canada for foreigners:

Job Boards

You can search for job postings on various job boards, such as Indeed, Monster, and Glassdoor. These websites allow you to filter your search by location, job title, and experience level.

Employment Agencies

You can also work with an employment agency that specializes in healthcare jobs. These agencies can help you find job openings that match your skills and experience.

What is the Salary for Pharmacy Technician Jobs in Canada for Foreigners?

The salary for pharmacy technician jobs in Canada varies depending on the province or territory where you work and your level of experience. According to PayScale, the average salary for a pharmacy technician in Canada is $42,000 per year.

Q: Can I work as a pharmacy technician in Canada without certification?

A: No, you must be certified by the Pharmacy Examining Board of Canada (PEBC) to work as a pharmacy technician in Canada.

Q: Can I take the PEBC certification exam if I live outside of Canada?

Q: Do I need to have work experience to be certified by the PEBC?
