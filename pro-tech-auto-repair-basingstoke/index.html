How to Know When Your Brakes Need Service

There are several signs that your brakes may need to be serviced, including:

  • Squeaking or grinding noises when you apply the brakes
  • Soft or spongy brake pedal
  • Pedal pulsation or vibration
  • Brake warning light on the dashboard

If you experience any of these symptoms, it's important to have your brakes inspected by a professional mechanic as soon as possible.
