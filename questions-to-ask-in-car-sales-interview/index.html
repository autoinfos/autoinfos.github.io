Questions To Ask In A Car Sales Interview

Written by Eveline May 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you preparing for a car sales interview? Do you want to know the relevant questions to ask during the interview? If your answer is yes, then you are in the right place.

Interviews can be stressful, especially when you’re not sure what to expect. But don't worry, we've got you covered. In this post, we will discuss the questions to ask in a car sales interview, so you can feel confident and prepared.

The primary goal of a car sales interview is to determine if you are a good fit for the job. The interviewer will ask you questions to gauge your knowledge of cars, sales experience, and customer service skills. They want to know if you can sell cars and make customers happy.

So, what are the questions you should ask during a car sales interview? Let’s find out.

What to Ask in a Car Sales Interview

Asking questions during the interview shows the interviewer that you are interested in the job and that you have done some research. Here are some questions you can ask:

1. What is the commission structure?

This question shows that you are interested in earning money and that you want to know how much you can make. The commission structure will vary by dealership, so it’s important to ask.

2. What training will be provided?

This question shows that you are interested in improving your skills and that you are willing to learn. Training is crucial in any job, so it’s important to know what kind of training will be provided.

3. How do you handle difficult customers?

This question shows that you are interested in providing excellent customer service and that you are prepared to handle difficult situations. It’s important to know how the dealership handles difficult customers so you can be prepared.

4. What is the culture like at the dealership?

This question shows that you are interested in the work environment and that you want to know if you will fit in. The culture of the dealership will affect your job satisfaction, so it’s important to ask.

Personal Experience

During my car sales interview, I asked about the commission structure and was pleased to find out that it was very generous. I also asked about the training that would be provided and was happy to hear that they had a comprehensive training program.

Conclusion of Questions to Ask in a Car Sales Interview

Asking questions during a car sales interview is essential to show that you are interested in the job and that you are prepared. The questions you ask will also help you determine if the dealership is a good fit for you. Remember, the interview is not just for the interviewer to determine if you are a good fit, it’s also for you to determine if the job is a good fit for you.

Question and Answer

Q: Can you ask about the salary during the interview?

A: It’s better to ask about the commission structure instead of the salary. The commission structure will give you a better idea of how much money you can make.

Q: What should you do if you don’t know the answer to a question?

A: It’s better to be honest and say that you don’t know the answer. However, you can also say that you are willing to learn and that you will find the answer for them.

Q: Is it important to ask about the work schedule?

A: Yes, it’s important to know what the work schedule will be like. This will help you plan your personal life and know what to expect.

Q: Should you ask about the benefits package?

A: Yes, it’s important to know what kind of benefits the dealership offers. This will help you determine if the job is a good fit for you.
