Small Engine Mechanic Salary Ontario What To Expect

Written by Eveline Aug 17, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you thinking of pursuing a career as a small engine mechanic in Ontario, but wondering about the salary prospects? If so, you are not alone. Many aspiring mechanics are concerned about the earning potential of this line of work. After all, it takes skill, training, and hard work to fix small engines, and you want to be compensated fairly for your efforts. In this article, we will explore the topic of small engine mechanic salary Ontario and provide insights into what you can expect in terms of compensation.

Being a small engine mechanic in Ontario comes with its own set of challenges. The cost of living in Ontario is relatively high, and you need to earn a decent salary to support yourself and your family. Unfortunately, small engine mechanic salary Ontario is not always on par with other skilled trades. In fact, the average salary for a small engine mechanic in Ontario is around $40,000 to $50,000 per year, which is lower than the national average. This can be a pain point for many mechanics who feel that they are not being paid their worth.

Despite the lower salary range, there are opportunities for small engine mechanics to increase their earning potential. For example, mechanics with specialized skills or certifications can command higher salaries. Additionally, those who work for larger companies or in higher-demand areas may earn more than those who work independently or in rural areas. It is also worth noting that small engine mechanic salary Ontario can vary depending on the type of engines you work on. For instance, those who specialize in repairing marine engines or small aircraft engines may earn more than those who focus on lawn mowers or snow blowers.

In summary, small engine mechanic salary Ontario may not be as high as other skilled trades, but there are opportunities to increase your earning potential. Specialized skills, certifications, and demand for your services can all impact your salary. It is also important to keep in mind that the type of engines you work on can also affect your salary.

What is the Average Small Engine Mechanic Salary Ontario?

I have been a small engine mechanic in Ontario for over a decade now, and I can tell you that the average salary ranges between $40,000 to $50,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on your location, experience, and type of engines you specialize in. When I started out, I was earning around $35,000 per year, but as I gained more experience and specialized in marine engines, my salary increased to around $60,000 per year.

How to Increase Small Engine Mechanic Salary Ontario?

If you want to increase your small engine mechanic salary Ontario, you can consider specializing in a particular type of engine, such as marine or aircraft engines. You can also pursue additional certifications, such as those offered by the Equipment and Engine Training Council (EETC), which can demonstrate your expertise and increase your value to employers. Additionally, you can look for opportunities to work for larger companies or in higher-demand areas, which may offer higher salaries.

Specializations That Can Increase Small Engine Mechanic Salary Ontario

As mentioned earlier, specializing in a particular type of engine can increase your small engine mechanic salary Ontario. Some popular specializations include:

- Marine engines
- Aircraft engines
- Snowmobile engines
- Motorcycle engines
- ATV engines

By specializing in one of these areas, you can become an expert in your field and command higher salaries.

How to Negotiate Small Engine Mechanic Salary Ontario?

If you are not satisfied with your small engine mechanic salary Ontario, you can consider negotiating with your employer. Before you do so, it is important to do your research and find out what the average salary is for your job in your area. You can also prepare a list of your accomplishments and skills, and use them to demonstrate your value to your employer. Remember to be professional and respectful during the negotiation process.

Conclusion of Small Engine Mechanic Salary Ontario

Small engine mechanic salary Ontario may not be the highest among skilled trades, but there are ways to increase your earning potential. Specializing in a particular type of engine, pursuing additional certifications, and working for larger companies or in higher-demand areas can all impact your salary. By keeping these factors in mind, you can make an informed decision about your career path as a small engine mechanic in Ontario.
