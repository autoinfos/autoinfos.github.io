Question and Answer

Q: What documents do I need to rent a car in UAE?

A: You will need a valid driving license, a passport or Emirates ID, and a credit card in your name to rent a car in UAE.

Q: Can I rent a car in UAE without a credit card?

A: Most car rental companies in UAE require a credit card in the renter's name as a security deposit. However, some companies may accept a debit card or cash deposit.

Q: Can I rent a car in UAE if I am under 25?

A: The minimum age requirement to rent a car in UAE is 21 years. However, some car rental companies may require the renter to be at least 25 years old.

Q: Is it mandatory to have car insurance when renting a car in UAE?

A: Yes, it is mandatory to have car insurance when renting a car in UAE. The rental company will provide basic insurance coverage, but you can also opt for additional insurance for extra protection.
