Top Schools For Automotive Design

Written by Oliver Jul 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you dream of designing the next generation of cars? Are you interested in the intersection of art and engineering? If so, pursuing a degree in automotive design may be the perfect path for you. But with so many schools offering automotive design programs, how do you choose the best one for your future career?

Choosing a school for automotive design can be overwhelming. You want to make sure you're getting the best education possible while also considering factors like location, cost, and curriculum. Plus, you want to know that you're getting a degree from a school that's respected in the industry.

So, what are the top schools for automotive design? Let's explore some of the best options and what they have to offer.

When it comes to the top schools for automotive design, there are a few that consistently rise to the top. These schools offer comprehensive programs that cover everything from design theory to the latest software and technology used in the industry. They also have strong connections to the automotive industry, which can help you land internships and job opportunities after graduation.

ArtCenter College of Design

ArtCenter College of Design is consistently ranked as one of the top schools for automotive design. Located in Pasadena, California, the school offers a Bachelor of Science in Transportation Design and a Master of Science in Transportation Systems and Design. The programs cover everything from sketching and ideation to 3D modeling and prototyping. Students also have access to state-of-the-art facilities and equipment, as well as opportunities to work on real-world projects with industry partners.

College for Creative Studies

Another top school for automotive design is the College for Creative Studies in Detroit, Michigan. The school offers a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Transportation Design and a Master of Fine Arts in Transportation Design. Students have access to a range of resources, including studio space, workshops, and a state-of-the-art digital fabrication lab. The school also has strong connections to the automotive industry, with partnerships with companies like General Motors and Ford.

Other Top Schools for Automotive Design

In addition to ArtCenter and College for Creative Studies, there are a few other schools worth considering for automotive design:

  • Lawrence Technological University in Southfield, Michigan
  • Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, New York
  • Umeå Institute of Design in Umeå, Sweden

How to Choose the Right School for You

When choosing a school for automotive design, it's important to consider your own goals and priorities. Think about what kind of program you want to pursue (e.g. Bachelor of Fine Arts vs. Bachelor of Science), what kind of curriculum you're interested in, and what kind of location and campus culture you're looking for. You should also consider factors like cost, scholarships and financial aid, and job placement rates after graduation.

My Personal Experience

As someone who has always been interested in cars and design, pursuing a degree in automotive design was a no-brainer for me. I attended ArtCenter College of Design and had an amazing experience. The curriculum was challenging but rewarding, and I learned so much about the design process and the automotive industry. I also had the opportunity to work on real-world projects with companies like Honda and Nissan, which was an incredible experience.

After graduating, I landed a job at an automotive design firm in Detroit, which was my dream job. I'm grateful for the education and experience I received at ArtCenter, which set me up for success in my career.

FAQs

What kind of jobs can I get with a degree in automotive design?

Graduates from automotive design programs can pursue careers as automotive designers, design engineers, product designers, and more. Some graduates also go on to start their own design firms or work as freelance designers.

Is it necessary to attend a top school for automotive design to have a successful career?

No, attending a top school for automotive design is not necessary to have a successful career. However, attending a respected school can help you build connections in the industry and gain valuable experience through internships and projects.

What software and technology will I learn in an automotive design program?

Automotive design programs typically cover a range of software and technology, including 3D modeling software like Alias and Rhino, rendering software like KeyShot, and prototyping tools like 3D printers and CNC machines.

What kind of salary can I expect with a degree in automotive design?

Salaries for automotive designers can vary depending on the company and location, but the median annual wage for industrial designers (which includes automotive designers) was $68,890 in 2020, according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics.

Conclusion of Top Schools for Automotive Design

Choosing a school for automotive design is an important decision that can set the foundation for your future career. By considering factors like curriculum, location, and industry connections, you can find a program that's right for you. Whether you choose to attend one of the top schools for automotive design or another respected institution, pursuing a degree in this field can be an exciting and rewarding experience.
