Question and Answer

Q: How long does it take to complete trade school for automotive mechanics?

A: The length of trade school for automotive mechanics programs can vary, but most programs take between 6 months and 2 years to complete.

Q: What types of jobs can I get with a degree in automotive mechanics?

A: With a degree in automotive mechanics, you can obtain jobs as an automotive technician, mechanic, service manager, or parts specialist, among others.

Q: Do I need any prior experience to enroll in trade school for automotive mechanics?

A: No prior experience is needed to enroll in trade school for automotive mechanics. However, having a passion for the industry and a willingness to learn is essential.
