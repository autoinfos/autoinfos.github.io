Question and Answer

Q: How often should I have my truck serviced?

A: It's recommended that you have your truck serviced every 10,000 miles or once a year, whichever comes first.

Q: How much does it cost to have my truck serviced?

A: The cost of servicing your truck can vary depending on the type of service and the shop you go to. It's a good idea to get quotes from several shops before making a decision.

Q: Can I do some of the maintenance myself?

A: Yes, there are several maintenance tasks that you can do yourself, such as checking the oil and inspecting the tires. However, more complex repairs should be left to a professional.

Q: What should I do if I have a breakdown on the road?

A: If you have a breakdown on the road, the first thing you should do is pull over to a safe location. From there, you can contact a roadside assistance service or a local repair shop for assistance.
