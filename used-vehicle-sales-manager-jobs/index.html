The Skills You Need to Succeed as a Used Vehicle Sales Manager

One of the most important skills you'll need as a used vehicle sales manager is strong leadership and communication skills. You'll be responsible for managing a team of salespeople, so you'll need to be able to motivate and inspire your team to achieve their sales targets.

One of the biggest benefits of being a used vehicle sales manager is the potential for a high income. As a sales manager, you'll typically earn a base salary plus commission on the vehicles you sell.
