Types of Jobs in Automobile Engineering

Automobile engineering is a broad field that includes many different types of jobs. Here are a few examples:

Automotive Engineer

Automotive engineers are responsible for designing and developing new vehicles. They work on everything from the engine and transmission to the suspension and brakes. Automotive engineers must have a strong understanding of mechanics, thermodynamics, and materials science.

Mechanical Engineer

Mechanical engineers work on the mechanical systems of vehicles. They design and develop engines, transmissions, and other mechanical components. They also work on the heating and cooling systems, as well as the suspension and steering systems.
