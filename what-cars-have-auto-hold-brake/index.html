What Is Auto Hold Brake and How Does It Work?

Auto hold brake is a feature that holds the car stationary without the driver having to press the brake pedal. This feature is activated when the car comes to a complete stop and the driver releases the brake pedal. Auto hold brake will keep the car stationary until the driver presses the accelerator pedal, at which point the brakes will release and the car will begin to move forward. This feature is particularly useful in stop-and-go traffic as it reduces the need for the driver to constantly press the brake pedal.
