What Do Automotive Technicians Make

Written by Eveline Jul 28, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you interested in a career in the automotive industry? Do you enjoy working with your hands and solving complex problems? If so, becoming an automotive technician might be the right path for you. However, before pursuing this career, it's important to understand what kind of salary you can expect. Read on to find out what automotive technicians make and how you can increase your earning potential.

As with any career, there are both advantages and challenges to being an automotive technician. While the work can be fulfilling and exciting, it can also be physically demanding and require long hours. Additionally, many technicians work on commission, which can make income unpredictable.

So, what do automotive technicians make? According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual salary for automotive service technicians and mechanics was $44,050 as of May 2020. However, this can vary depending on factors such as experience, location, and certification. ASE certified technicians, for example, can earn higher salaries due to their expertise and training.

In order to increase your earning potential as an automotive technician, there are several steps you can take. Firstly, gaining experience and becoming proficient in a wide range of repairs and diagnostic procedures can make you a valuable asset to your employer. Additionally, pursuing certification through organizations like ASE can demonstrate your knowledge and expertise to potential employers.

What Do Automotive Technicians Make: A Personal Experience

As someone who has worked in the automotive industry for over a decade, I can attest to the fact that being an automotive technician can be a rewarding and challenging career. When I first started out, I was making around $30,000 per year. However, as I gained experience and became certified in more areas, my income steadily increased. Now, I make over $60,000 per year and have the satisfaction of knowing that I am helping people stay safe on the road.

What Do Automotive Technicians Make: Tips for Increasing Your Salary

If you're looking to increase your earning potential as an automotive technician, there are several steps you can take. Firstly, gaining experience and becoming proficient in a wide range of repairs and diagnostic procedures can make you a valuable asset to your employer. Additionally, pursuing certification through organizations like ASE can demonstrate your knowledge and expertise to potential employers.

Becoming ASE Certified

If you're serious about increasing your earning potential as an automotive technician, becoming ASE certified is a great way to demonstrate your expertise and knowledge. ASE certification is recognized throughout the industry and can make you a more valuable asset to your employer.

Specializing in a Niche Area

Another way to increase your earning potential as an automotive technician is to specialize in a niche area. For example, if you become an expert in hybrid or electric vehicle repairs, you can command higher salaries due to your unique skillset.

What Do Automotive Technicians Make: A Final Word

Overall, while the salary for automotive technicians can vary depending on a range of factors, it is a career that offers both challenges and rewards. By gaining experience, pursuing certification, and specializing in a niche area, you can increase your earning potential and enjoy a fulfilling career in the automotive industry.
