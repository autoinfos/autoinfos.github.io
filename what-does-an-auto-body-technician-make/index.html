What Does An Auto Body Technician Make Find Out Here

Written by Christine May 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you interested in becoming an auto body technician? Or are you curious about how much money they make? Either way, you're in the right place. In this blog post, we'll dive into everything you need to know about what an auto body technician makes.

Being an auto body technician can be a challenging job, from repairing damaged cars to repainting them. But the biggest concern most people have is how much they can earn from this profession. After all, you want to make sure that you're making a good living and able to support yourself.

So, what does an auto body technician make? According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual salary for auto body technicians in the United States is $43,580. This means that half of the technicians earn more than this amount, while the other half earns less than this amount.

In addition to the median salary, the amount you can make as an auto body technician can vary based on several factors. These include your experience level, location, and the company you work for. For example, auto body technicians in big cities tend to earn more than those in rural areas. And technicians with more experience and training may also earn higher salaries.

My Experience as an Auto Body Technician

As someone who has worked as an auto body technician for over ten years, I can say that the job can be both rewarding and challenging. I started out with a salary of around $30,000, but as I gained more experience and became more skilled, my salary increased to over $50,000.

One of the things that I enjoy most about being an auto body technician is the creative aspect of the job. I love being able to take a damaged car and transform it into something beautiful. Plus, it's always satisfying to see a happy customer drive away in a car that looks as good as new.

Factors That Affect an Auto Body Technician's Salary

As we mentioned earlier, there are several factors that can affect how much an auto body technician makes. Here are a few of the most important ones:

Experience Level

The more experience you have as an auto body technician, the higher your salary is likely to be. This is because experienced technicians are more skilled and can handle more complex repairs.

Location

The location where you work can also affect your salary. Auto body technicians in large cities tend to earn more than those in smaller towns or rural areas. This is because the cost of living tends to be higher in big cities.

Training and Education

Having advanced training and education can also increase your earning potential as an auto body technician. For example, getting certified in a specific area like painting or welding can help you stand out from other technicians.

Conclusion of What an Auto Body Technician Makes

Now that you know what an auto body technician makes, you can decide if this is the right career for you. While the salary may not be the highest, it can be a rewarding job for those who are skilled and passionate about cars. Plus, with the right training and experience, you can earn a good living and build a successful career in this industry.
