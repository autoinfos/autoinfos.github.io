Being an AutoZone Manager can be a challenging job, especially when it comes to managing a team of employees and ensuring that the store runs smoothly. However, one of the biggest concerns for most people who are considering this job is how much they can expect to earn.

According to Glassdoor, the average salary for an AutoZone Manager is around $45,000 per year. However, this can vary based on factors such as experience, location, and the size of the store. Factors that Affect AutoZone Manager Salary

Several factors can affect an AutoZone Manager's salary, including:

  • Experience: Those who have been with the company for longer are likely to earn more.
  • Location: Managers in larger cities or areas with a higher cost of living may earn more than those in smaller towns or rural areas.
  • Store size: Managers of larger stores may earn more than those of smaller stores.
