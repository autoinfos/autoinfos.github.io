What Is The Salary Of Civil Engineer In Dubai

Written by Aprilia Oct 12, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you contemplating a career as a civil engineer in Dubai? One of the critical questions you may have is how much you can earn as a civil engineer in Dubai. The answer to this question is not straightforward as there are several factors that affect the salary of civil engineers in Dubai.

Generally, the salary of civil engineers in Dubai is higher than other countries due to the high demand for their services. However, the salary varies based on several factors such as years of experience, education level, industry, and company size.

The average salary of a civil engineer in Dubai is around AED 100,000 per year. However, this figure can range from AED 60,000 to AED 200,000 based on the factors mentioned above.

Factors that Affect the Salary of Civil Engineer in Dubai

Years of Experience

As with most professions, the salary of civil engineers in Dubai increases as they gain more experience. Junior civil engineers with less than five years of experience can earn between AED 60,000 to AED 90,000, while senior civil engineers with over ten years of experience can earn up to AED 200,000.

Education Level

Civil engineers with higher education levels such as a master's degree or PhD can earn a higher salary than those with just a bachelor's degree.

Personal Experience

As a civil engineer in Dubai, I have experienced the impact of the factors mentioned above on my salary. Having worked in the industry for over ten years and with a master's degree, my salary is around AED 180,000 per year.

Industry and Company Size

The industry and company size also play a significant role in determining the salary of civil engineers in Dubai. Civil engineers working in the construction industry or for large multinational companies generally earn higher salaries than those working for small companies.

Question and Answer

Q: Can civil engineers in Dubai earn more than AED 200,000 per year?

A: Yes, civil engineers with exceptional skills and experience can earn more than AED 200,000 per year.

Q: Is the demand for civil engineers in Dubai expected to increase in the future?

A: Yes, with several large infrastructure projects planned in Dubai, the demand for civil engineers is expected to increase in the future.

Q: Can civil engineers in Dubai earn bonuses and benefits?

A: Yes, civil engineers in Dubai can earn bonuses and benefits such as health insurance, travel allowances, and housing allowances.

Q: Are civil engineers in Dubai required to have any specific certifications?

A: Yes, civil engineers in Dubai are required to have a valid Dubai Municipality License to practice in Dubai.

Conclusion of What is Salary of Civil Engineer in Dubai

In conclusion, the salary of civil engineers in Dubai is influenced by several factors such as years of experience, education level, industry, and company size. The average salary of a civil engineer in Dubai is around AED 100,000 per year, but this can range from AED 60,000 to AED 200,000. As the demand for civil engineers is expected to increase in the future, the salary is also expected to increase.
