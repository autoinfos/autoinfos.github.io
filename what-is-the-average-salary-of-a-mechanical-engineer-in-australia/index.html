What Is The Average Salary Of A Mechanical Engineer In Australia

Written by Bardi Jun 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you considering a career in mechanical engineering in Australia? One of the most important factors to consider is the average salary for this profession. The salary you can expect to earn will depend on a variety of factors, including your experience, education, and location. In this article, we'll explore the average salary of a mechanical engineer in Australia.

The average salary for a mechanical engineer in Australia is approximately AU$80,000 per year. However, this can vary greatly depending on your location and level of experience. Entry-level mechanical engineers can expect to earn around AU$55,000 per year, while those with more experience can earn up to AU$120,000 per year. Additionally, mechanical engineers in larger cities, such as Sydney and Melbourne, tend to earn higher salaries than those in smaller towns and rural areas.

In summary, the average salary for a mechanical engineer in Australia is around AU$80,000 per year. However, this can vary depending on a number of factors, including your experience and location.

What Factors Affect the Salary of a Mechanical Engineer in Australia?

When it comes to determining the salary of a mechanical engineer in Australia, there are several factors to consider. One of the most important factors is your level of experience in the field. Entry-level engineers can expect to earn less than those with more experience. Additionally, the location where you work can have a significant impact on your salary. Mechanical engineers in larger cities tend to earn more than those in smaller towns and rural areas.

My Personal Experience as a Mechanical Engineer in Australia

As a mechanical engineer in Australia with several years of experience, I can attest to the fact that location plays a major role in determining your salary. I have found that working in larger cities, such as Sydney and Melbourne, tends to result in higher salaries. Additionally, the industry you work in can also affect your salary. Mechanical engineers who work in the mining and oil and gas industries tend to earn higher salaries than those in other industries.

What Education is Required to Become a Mechanical Engineer in Australia?

In order to become a mechanical engineer in Australia, you will need to obtain a bachelor's degree in mechanical engineering from an accredited university. Additionally, many employers prefer candidates who have obtained a master's degree in mechanical engineering or a related field. It is also important to obtain professional certification from organizations such as Engineers Australia in order to demonstrate your expertise in the field.

How to Increase Your Salary as a Mechanical Engineer in Australia

If you are looking to increase your salary as a mechanical engineer in Australia, there are several steps you can take. First, consider obtaining additional education or certifications in your field. This can demonstrate your expertise and make you a more attractive candidate to potential employers. Additionally, consider working in a high-demand industry, such as mining or oil and gas. Finally, be willing to relocate to larger cities where salaries tend to be higher.

What is the Job Outlook for Mechanical Engineers in Australia?

The job outlook for mechanical engineers in Australia is positive, with steady growth expected in the coming years. According to the Australian Government's Job Outlook website, there are currently around 26,000 mechanical engineers employed in Australia, and this number is expected to grow to around 28,000 by 2024.
