Understanding the Pain Points of What is the Average Salary of an Auto Mechanic

One of the main pain points of considering a career as an auto mechanic is the uncertainty around salary. Answering the Target of What is the Average Salary of an Auto Mechanic

According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual salary for auto mechanics was $42,090 as of May 2020. This means that half of all auto mechanics earned more than this amount, while the other half earned less. The lowest 10 percent of auto mechanics earned less than $24,590 per year, while the highest 10 percent earned more than $69,610 per year.

Summary of What is the Average Salary of an Auto Mechanic and Related Keywords

In summary, the average salary for an auto mechanic is $42,090 per year, but can range from $24,590 to $69,610 depending on experience and other factors. It's important to note that salaries can vary depending on location, employer, and other factors as well.

Going Deeper into What is the Average Salary of an Auto Mechanic and Related Keywords

How to Increase Your Salary as an Auto Mechanic

If you're looking to increase your salary as an auto mechanic, there are a few things you can do.
