The average salary for an engineer in Canada varies depending on the type of engineering and the level of experience. According to the Canadian Government, the median salary for electrical and electronics engineers is $91,520 per year, while civil, mechanical, and chemical engineers earn a median salary of $97,280 per year. Senior engineers with more than ten years of experience can earn upwards of $150,000 per year.
