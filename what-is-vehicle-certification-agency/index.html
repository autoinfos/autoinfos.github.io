Answering the Target of Vehicle Certification Agency

The Vehicle Certification Agency (VCA) is a UK-based government agency responsible for regulating and certifying vehicles and their components. The agency was established in 2003 and is a part of the Department for Transport.

The primary role of the VCA is to ensure that all vehicles and their components meet the necessary safety and environmental standards. They also provide certification services to manufacturers, importers, and individual vehicle owners.
