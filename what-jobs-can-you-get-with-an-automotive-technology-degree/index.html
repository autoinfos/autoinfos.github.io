What Jobs Can You Get With An Automotive Technology Degree

Written by Bardi Sep 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you interested in cars and technology? Do you want to work in the automotive industry but not as a mechanic? Then an automotive technology degree might be for you. However, you may be wondering what jobs you can get with this degree.

Having an automotive technology degree opens doors to a variety of careers in the automotive industry. This degree provides you with the skills and knowledge to work as a technician, engineer, or manager in the automotive field.

As a technician, you can work in automotive repair and maintenance. You can also specialize in areas such as brakes, transmissions, or engines. As an engineer, you can design and develop new automotive technologies such as electric or hybrid cars. As a manager, you can oversee the operations of an automotive business or work in sales and marketing.

In summary, an automotive technology degree can lead to careers as a technician, engineer, or manager in the automotive industry. With this degree, you can specialize in various areas such as repair and maintenance, design and development, and sales and marketing.

Technician

After completing your automotive technology degree, you may choose to work as a technician. As a technician, you will diagnose and repair vehicles. You may specialize in specific areas such as brakes, transmissions, or engines. As a technician, you may work in automotive repair shops, dealerships, or manufacturing plants. You may also have the opportunity to work on different types of vehicles such as cars, trucks, or motorcycles.

Engineer

If you have an interest in designing and developing new automotive technologies, then becoming an engineer may be a good fit for you. As an engineer, you will be responsible for creating new products or improving existing ones. You may work on projects such as electric or hybrid cars, self-driving cars, or fuel-efficient engines. You may also work for automotive manufacturers, suppliers, or research institutions.

Manager

With an automotive technology degree, you can also work as a manager in the automotive industry. As a manager, you may oversee the operations of an automotive business such as a repair shop or dealership. You may also work in sales and marketing, promoting and selling vehicles or automotive products. In this role, you will need to have strong communication and leadership skills.

How to Start Your Career in Automotive Technology

If you are interested in pursuing a career in automotive technology, the first step is to obtain an automotive technology degree. You can find programs at community colleges, vocational schools, or universities. Some programs may also offer internships or apprenticeships, providing you with hands-on experience in the field.

After completing your degree, you can start looking for job opportunities. You can search for jobs in online job boards, company websites, or through networking. You may also consider obtaining certification in specific areas such as brakes, transmissions, or engines, to enhance your skills and become more competitive in the job market.

Question and Answer

Q: What skills do I need to work in automotive technology?

A: To work in automotive technology, you need to have strong analytical, technical, and problem-solving skills. You also need to be detail-oriented and have good communication skills to work with customers and colleagues.

Q: What is the job outlook for automotive technicians?

A: According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, employment of automotive service technicians and mechanics is projected to grow 4 percent from 2019 to 2029, about as fast as the average for all occupations.

Q: Do I need certification to work as an automotive technician?

A: Although certification is not required to work as an automotive technician, it can enhance your skills and job opportunities. You can obtain certification through organizations such as the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence (ASE).

Q: What is the average salary for someone with an automotive technology degree?

A: According to PayScale, the average salary for someone with an automotive technology degree is $56,000 per year.

Conclusion of What Jobs Can You Get with an Automotive Technology Degree

Having an automotive technology degree can lead to a variety of careers in the automotive industry such as technician, engineer, or manager. This degree provides you with the skills and knowledge to work in areas such as repair and maintenance, design and development, and sales and marketing. If you are interested in pursuing a career in automotive technology, obtaining an automotive technology degree and obtaining certification can enhance your skills and job opportunities.
