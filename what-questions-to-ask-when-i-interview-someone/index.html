What Questions To Ask When I Interview Someone

Written by Eveline Jun 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

As an interior designer, you want to make sure you hire the right person for the job. But how do you know who to hire? One way is to conduct a thorough interview process. Asking the right questions can reveal a lot about a candidate's experience, skills, and work ethic. In this blog post, we'll discuss what questions to ask when I interview someone.

Interviewing candidates can be a daunting task, but it's essential to ensure you choose the right person for the job. Asking the wrong questions can lead to hiring someone who doesn't have the necessary skills or experience to complete the project, which can cause delays or even compromise the overall design. Therefore, it's crucial to ask the right questions and ensure you get the information you need to make an informed decision.

The questions you ask will depend on the role you're hiring for and the specific needs of your project. However, some general questions can help you get a better understanding of the candidate's experience, work style, and ability to meet deadlines. Below are some general questions that can help you get started:

What is your experience in the industry?

This question can help you get a better understanding of the candidate's background and how it aligns with the needs of your project. Ask about their experience working on similar projects and their approach to solving design challenges. Look for candidates who have experience working on projects similar to yours, as they are more likely to have the necessary skills and knowledge to complete the job.

What is your design philosophy?

As an interior designer, you want to ensure that the candidate's design philosophy aligns with yours. Ask about their approach to design, their style preferences, and their design process. Look for candidates who have a similar design philosophy to yours, as they are more likely to understand and execute your vision for the project.

How do you handle tight deadlines?

Meeting deadlines is crucial in the interior design industry, and you want to ensure that the candidate can handle the pressure of tight deadlines. Ask about their experience working under tight deadlines and how they manage their time to ensure they meet deadlines. Look for candidates who have a track record of meeting deadlines, as this shows they have the necessary time-management skills to complete the project on time.

What is your experience working with clients?

Working with clients is a significant part of the interior design process, and you want to ensure that the candidate has the necessary skills to communicate effectively with clients. Ask about their experience working with clients, how they handle difficult situations, and their approach to client communication. Look for candidates who have excellent communication skills and are comfortable working with clients to ensure a successful project outcome.

Personal Experience:

When I interview potential candidates, I make sure to ask about their experience working on projects similar to mine. I want to ensure that they have the necessary skills and knowledge to complete the job to my standards. I also ask about their design philosophy to ensure that it aligns with mine. It's essential to have a team that shares your design vision to ensure a cohesive final design.

Conclusion of What Questions to Ask When I Interview Someone:

Interviewing candidates is a crucial part of the interior design process, and asking the right questions is essential to ensure you hire the right person for the job. Questions about experience, design philosophy, time-management skills, and client communication can help you get a better understanding of the candidate's abilities and work style. By asking the right questions, you can ensure a successful project outcome and a team that shares your design vision.
