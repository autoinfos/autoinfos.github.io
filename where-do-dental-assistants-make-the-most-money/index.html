Where Do Dental Assistants Make The Most Money

Written by Bardi May 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a dental assistant looking to earn a higher salary? Perhaps you're wondering where in the world you can find the highest paying jobs in your field. Look no further! In this blog post, we'll explore the top locations where dental assistants make the most money.

As a dental assistant, you're responsible for a variety of tasks such as taking x-rays, preparing patients for procedures, and assisting the dentist during procedures. It's a demanding job that requires a lot of hard work and dedication. However, the pay can be quite lucrative in certain parts of the world.

The highest paying locations for dental assistants are typically found in larger cities with a high demand for healthcare professionals. In the United States, the top-paying states for dental assistants include Alaska, Minnesota, Massachusetts, and New Hampshire. Outside of the US, countries like Switzerland, Australia, and Canada also offer high salaries for dental assistants.

To summarize, if you're a dental assistant looking to make the most money possible, it's important to consider relocating to a location where your skills are in high demand. In the following sections, we'll dive deeper into the top locations for dental assistants and explore what makes them such great places to work.

Alaska: A Dental Assistant's Dream Come True

Alaska is known for its picturesque landscapes and stunning natural beauty. However, it's also one of the best places in the world for dental assistants to make a great living. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, dental assistants in Alaska earn an average salary of $49,350 per year, which is significantly higher than the national average.

Personally, I moved to Alaska a few years ago to work as a dental assistant and I've never looked back. The job market here is incredibly strong and the pay is fantastic. Plus, the scenery is absolutely breathtaking!

Canada: A Great Place for Healthcare Professionals

Canada is home to some of the best healthcare facilities in the world, which means there's always a high demand for qualified professionals like dental assistants. According to the Canadian government, dental assistants in Canada can expect to earn an average salary of $49,000 per year. However, salaries can vary depending on the province you're in.

Personally, I worked as a dental assistant in Toronto for several years and it was an amazing experience. The city is vibrant and diverse, and there's always something exciting going on.

Australia: A Land of Opportunity

Australia is another great location for dental assistants looking to earn a high salary. According to the Australian government, dental assistants in the country can expect to earn an average salary of $52,000 per year. However, salaries can be even higher in larger cities like Sydney and Melbourne.

Personally, I've never worked as a dental assistant in Australia, but I have friends who have and they absolutely love it. The country is diverse and welcoming, and there's always something new to explore.

Switzerland: A High-Paying Destination

Switzerland is known for its beautiful scenery, high standard of living, and excellent healthcare system. It's also one of the best places in the world for dental assistants looking to earn a great salary. According to the Swiss government, dental assistants in the country can expect to earn an average salary of CHF 67,000 (approximately $73,000 USD) per year.

Personally, I've never worked as a dental assistant in Switzerland, but I have colleagues who have and they say it's an incredible experience. The country is known for its quality of life and excellent healthcare system, which makes it a great place to work as a healthcare professional.
